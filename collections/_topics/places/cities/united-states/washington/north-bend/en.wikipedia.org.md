**North Bend** is a city in [King County](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/King_County,_Washington), [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)), [United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States), on the outskirts of the [Seattle](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seattle) metropolitan area. The population was 7,461 at the 2020 census.
