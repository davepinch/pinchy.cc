**Mare Crisium** /ˈkrɪsiəm/ (Latin crisium, the "**Sea of Crises**") is a lunar mare located in the Moon's Crisium basin, just northeast of Mare Tranquillitatis. Mare Crisium is a basin of Nectarian age.
