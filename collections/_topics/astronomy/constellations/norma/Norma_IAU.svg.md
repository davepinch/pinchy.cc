Faint constellation SW of Scorpius. Asterism: quadrangle: Eta, Delta, Epsilon, Gamma 1,2. NE corner Lambda, Mu & QU. South: Kappa, Zeta, Iota 1,2. Lacaille here lauded the surveyors' "right-angle" (Latin: Norma).
