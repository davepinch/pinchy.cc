Wyrd Sisters is [Terry Pratchett](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Terry_Pratchett)'s sixth [Discworld](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Discworld) novel, published in 1988. It re-introduces Granny Weatherwax of [Equal Rites](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Equal_Rites).
