> ***Frederick the Great Playing the Flute at Sanssouci*** or ***The Flute Concert*** is an 1852 oil on canvas history painting by the German painter [Adolph Menzel](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Adolph_Menzel). It depicts [Frederick the Great](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Frederick_the_Great), King of Prussia playing the flute at an evening concert at Sanssouci and is now in the Alte Nationalgalerie in Berlin.
