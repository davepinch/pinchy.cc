In physics and cosmology, the **mathematical universe hypothesis** (**MUH**), also known as the **ultimate ensemble theory** and **struogony** (from mathematical structure, Latin: stru≈ç), is a speculative "theory of everything" (TOE) proposed by cosmologist Max Tegmark.
