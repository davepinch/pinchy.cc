**Iowa** (/ˈaɪəwə/) is a state in the [upper](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Upper_Midwest) [Midwestern](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Midwestern_United_States) region of the [United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States), bordered by the [Mississippi River](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mississippi_River) to the east and the Missouri River and Big Sioux River to the west. It is bordered by six states: [Wisconsin](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wisconsin) to the northeast, [Illinois](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Illinois) to the east and southeast, [Missouri](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Missouri) to the south, [Nebraska](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nebraska) to the west, [South Dakota](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/South_Dakota) to the northwest, and [Minnesota](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Minnesota) to the north.
