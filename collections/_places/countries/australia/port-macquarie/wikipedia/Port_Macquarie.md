**Port Macquarie** is a coastal town in the local government area of Port Macquarie-Hastings. It is located on the Mid North Coast of [New South Wales]({% include cc-url for="New South Wales - Wikipedia" %}), [Australia]({% include cc-url for="Australia - Wikipedia" %}), about 390 km (242 mi) north of Sydney, and 570 km (354 mi) south of Brisbane. The town is located on the Tasman Sea coast, at the mouth of the Hastings River, and at the eastern end of the Oxley Highway (B56). The town with its suburbs had a population of 47,973 in June 2018.
