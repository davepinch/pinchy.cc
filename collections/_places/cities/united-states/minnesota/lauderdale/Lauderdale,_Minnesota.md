**Lauderdale** is a city in [Ramsey County](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ramsey_County,_Minnesota), [Minnesota](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Minnesota), [United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States). The population was 2,379 at the 2010 census. Lauderdale is part of the Minneapolisâ€“St. Paul metropolitan area and is surrounded by [Minneapolis](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Minneapolis), [St. Paul](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Saint_Paul,_Minnesota), [Roseville](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Roseville,_Minnesota), and [Falcon Heights](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Falcon_Heights,_Minnesota). Along with Roseville, it is one of two cities to touch both Minneapolis and St Paul.
