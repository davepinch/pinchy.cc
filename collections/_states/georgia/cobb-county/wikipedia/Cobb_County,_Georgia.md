**Cobb County** is a county in the U.S. state of [Georgia]({% include cc-url for="Georgia (U.S. state) - Wikipedia" %}), located in the Atlanta metropolitan area in the north central portion of the state. As of 2020 Census, the population was 766,149. Its county seat and largest city is Marietta.
