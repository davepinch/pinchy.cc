**Georgia** is a state in the Southeastern region of the United States, bordered to the north by [Tennessee]({% include cc-url for="Tennessee - Wikipedia" %}) and [North Carolina]({% include cc-url for="North Carolina - Wikipedia" %}); to the northeast by [South Carolina]({% include cc-url for="South Carolina - Wikipedia" %}); to the southeast by the Atlantic Ocean; to the south by [Florida]({% include cc-url for="Florida - Wikipedia" %}); and to the west by [Alabama]({% include cc-url for="Alabama - Wikipedia" %}). Georgia is the 24th-largest state in area and 8th most populous of the 50 United States. Its 2020 population was 10,711,908, according to the U.S. Census Bureau. [Atlanta]({% include cc-url for="Atlanta - Wikipedia" %}), a "beta(+)" global city, is both the state's capital and its largest city. The Atlanta metropolitan area, with a population of more than 6 million people in 2020, is the 9th most populous metropolitan area in the United States and contains about 57% of Georgia's entire population.
