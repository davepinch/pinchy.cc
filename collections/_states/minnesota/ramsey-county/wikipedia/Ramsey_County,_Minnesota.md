**Ramsey County** is a county located in the U.S. state of [Minnesota]({% include cc-url for="Minnesota - Wikipedia" %}). As of the 2020 census, the population was 552,352, making it the second-most populous county in Minnesota. Its county seat and largest city is [Saint Paul]({% include cc-url for="Saint Paul, Minnesota - Wikipedia" %}), the state capital and the twin city of [Minneapolis]({% include cc-url for="Minneapolis - Wikipedia" %}). The county was founded in 1849 and is named for Alexander Ramsey, the first governor of the Minnesota Territory.
