**Lake Phalen** is an urban lake located in [Saint Paul]({% include cc-url for="Saint Paul, Minnesota - Wikipedia" %}), Minnesota and in its suburb of [Maplewood]({% include cc-url for="Maplewood, Minnesota - Wikipedia" %}). It is one of the largest lakes in Saint Paul and is the centerpiece of the Phalen Regional Park System. The lake drains into the [Mississippi River]({% include cc-url for="Mississippi River - Wikipedia" %}) after traveling through Phalen Creek. The lake and surrounding 494-acre (2.00 km2) park receive around 500,000 visitors each year.
