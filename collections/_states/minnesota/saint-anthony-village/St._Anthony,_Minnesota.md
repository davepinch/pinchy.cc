**St. Anthony**, also known as **Saint Anthony Village**, is a city in [Hennepin]({% include cc-url for="Hennepin County, Minnesota - Wikipedia" %}) and [Ramsey]({% include cc-url for="Ramsey County, Minnesota - Wikipedia" %}) counties in the U.S. state of [Minnesota]({% include cc-url for="Minnesota - Wikipedia" %}). At the 2010 census the population was 8,226, of whom 5,156 lived in the larger Hennepin County part of the city and 3,070 in the Ramsey County part. The city is run by a five-member council consisting of a mayor and four council members who serve four-year terms.
