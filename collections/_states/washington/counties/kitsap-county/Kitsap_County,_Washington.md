**Kitsap County** is located in the U.S. state of [Washington]({% include cc-url for="Washington (state) - Wikipedia" %}). As of the 2020 census, its population was 275,611. Its county seat is Port Orchard, and its largest city is [Bremerton]({% include cc-url for="Bremerton, Washington - Wikipedia" %}). The county was formed out of [King County]({% include cc-url for="King County, Washington - Wikipedia" %}) and Jefferson County on January 16, 1857, and is named for Chief Kitsap of the Suquamish Tribe. Originally named Slaughter County, it was soon renamed.
