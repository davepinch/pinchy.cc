**Lake Washington** is a large freshwater lake adjacent to the city of [Seattle]({% include cc-url for="Seattle - Wikipedia" %}). It is the largest lake in [King County]({% include cc-url for="King County, Washington - Wikipedia" %}) and the second largest natural lake in the state of [Washington]({% include cc-url for="Washington (state) - Wikipedia" %}), after Lake Chelan. It borders the cities of Seattle on the west, Bellevue and Kirkland on the east, Renton on the south and Kenmore on the north, and encloses Mercer Island. The lake is fed by the [Sammamish River]({% include cc-url for="Sammamish River - Wikipedia" %}) at its north end and the Cedar River at its south.
