**Bainbridge Island** is a city and island in [Kitsap County]({% include cc-url for="Kitsap County, Washington - Wikipedia" %}), Washington. It is located in Puget Sound. The population was 23,025 at the 2010 census and an estimated 25,298 in 2019, making Bainbridge Island the second largest city in Kitsap County.
