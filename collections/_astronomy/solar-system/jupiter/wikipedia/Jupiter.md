**Jupiter** is the fifth planet from the [Sun]({% include cc-url for="Sun - Wikipedia" %}) and the largest in the [Solar System]({% include cc-url for="Solar System - Wikipedia" %}). It is a gas giant with a mass more than two and a half times that of all the other planets in the Solar System combined, but slightly less than one-thousandth the mass of the Sun. Jupiter is the third brightest natural object in the Earth's night sky after the [Moon]({% include cc-url for="Moon - Wikipedia" %}) and [Venus]({% include cc-url for="Venus - Wikipedia" %}), and it has been observed since prehistoric times. It was named after the Roman god [Jupiter]({% include cc-url for="Jupiter (mythology) - Wikipedia" %}), the king of the gods.
