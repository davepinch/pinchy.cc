**Chicago** (/ʃɪˈkɑːɡoʊ/ *shih-KAH-goh*, locally also /ʃɪˈkɔːɡoʊ/ *shih-KAW-goh*) is the most populous city in the U.S. state of Illinois, and the third-most populous in the United States, following New York City and Los Angeles. With a population of 2,746,388 in the 2020 census, it is the most populous city in the Midwestern United States. Chicago, the county seat of Cook County (the second-most populous U.S. county), is the center of the Chicago metropolitan area, one of the largest in the world.
