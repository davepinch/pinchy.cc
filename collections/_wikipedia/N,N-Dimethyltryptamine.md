***N,N*-Dimethyltryptamine** (**DMT** or ***N,N*-DMT**, **SPL026**) is a substituted tryptamine that occurs in many plants and animals, including human beings, and which is both a derivative and a structural analog of tryptamine. It is used as a recreational psychedelic drug and prepared by various cultures for ritual purposes as an entheogen.
