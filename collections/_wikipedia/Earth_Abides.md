**Earth Abides** is a 1949 American post-apocalyptic [science fiction]({% include cc-url for="Science fiction - Wikipedia" %}) novel by George R. Stewart. The novel tells the story of the fall of civilization from deadly disease and the emergence of a new culture with simpler tools. Set in the 1940s in Berkeley, [California]({% include cc-url for="California - Wikipedia" %}), the story is told by Isherwood Williams, who emerges from isolation in the mountains to find almost everyone dead.
