**Franklin Delano Roosevelt** (/ˈdɛlənoʊ ˈroʊzəvɛlt, -vəlt/ *DEL-ə-noh ROH-zə-velt*, -⁠vəlt; January 30, 1882 – April 12, 1945), commonly known as FDR, was an American statesman and political leader who served as the 32nd president of the [United States]({% include cc-url for="United States - Wikipedia" %}) from 1933 until his death in 1945. He previously served as the 44th governor of New York from 1929 to 1933, the Assistant Secretary of the Navy from 1913 to 1920, and a member of the New York State Senate from 1911 to 1913.
