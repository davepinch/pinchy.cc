**Thomas Eugene Robbins** (born July 22, 1932) is a best-selling and prolific American novelist. His most notable works are "seriocomedies" (also known as "comedy drama"), such as *Even Cowgirls Get the Blues*. Tom Robbins has lived in [La Conner]({% include cc-url for="La Conner, Washington - Wikipedia" %}), Washington since 1970, where he has written nine best-selling books. His latest work, published in 2014, is *Tibetan Peach Pie*, which is a self-declared "un-memoir". *Even Cowgirls Get the Blues* has been adapted into a movie that shares the same name by Gus Van Sant in 1993.
