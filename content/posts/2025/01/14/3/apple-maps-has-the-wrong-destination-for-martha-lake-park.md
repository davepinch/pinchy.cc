title: "Apple Maps has the wrong destination for Martha Lake Park"
apple maps of: Martha Lake Park
details:
  - Apple Maps will bring you to the front of the park along 164th St SE. You cannot park along 164th. Instead turn north on 6th Ave W or alternately turn north on Larch Way for a drive around the lake. The parking lot for Martha Lake Park is along East Shore Drive.
