memory: >-
  I saw this film from the backseat of a car in 1979 at a drive-in theater in Minnesota. Before the film, I remember playing outside at a playground in our apartment complex. My mom came out and said we were going to see the movie. I don't recall anything about the movie except for sitting in the dark back seat. Years later I watched the movie from a VCR tape in Maplewood.
