title: "Lacamas Heritage Trail (David Pinch)"
memory: >-
  Mary and I had driven to Vancouver (Washington) for a Amber Brick deliver. After delivering, I looked on the map to find a local park we could explore. We started from the north-side trailhead, first passing through an open area (I disctinctly remember the electrical pilons) and the entering a forested trail along Lacamas Creek. The trail has a number of adjacent properties with gates and docks, which I thought was nice for them to be so close to a natural area.
