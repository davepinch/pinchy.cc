  **NGC 185** (also known as **Caldwell 18**) is a dwarf spheroidal galaxy located 2.08 million light-years from [Earth](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Earth/), appearing in the constellation [Cassiopeia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cassiopeia_(constellation)/). It is a member of the [Local group](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Local_Group/), and is a satellite of the [Andromeda Galaxy](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Andromeda_Galaxy/) (M31).
