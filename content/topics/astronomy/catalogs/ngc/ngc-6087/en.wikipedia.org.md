  **NGC 6087** (also known as **Caldwell 89** or the **S Normae Cluster**) is an open cluster of 40 or more stars centered on the Cepheid variable S Normae in the constellation [Norma](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Norma_(constellation)/). At a distance of about 3500 ly and covering a field of almost one quarter of a degree, the stars range from seventh- to eleventh-magnitude, the brightest being 6.5 magnitude S Normae. The aggregate visual magnitude of the cluster is about 5.4.
