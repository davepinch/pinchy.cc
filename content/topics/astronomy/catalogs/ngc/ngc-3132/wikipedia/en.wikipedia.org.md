  **NGC 3132** (also known as the **Eight-Burst Nebula**, the **Southern Ring Nebula**, or **Caldwell 74**) is a bright and extensively studied planetary nebula in the constellation [Vela](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vela_(constellation)/). Its distance from [Earth](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Earth/) is estimated at 613 pc or 2,000 light-years.
