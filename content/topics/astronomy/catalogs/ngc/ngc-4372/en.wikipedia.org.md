  **NGC 4372** (also known as **Caldwell 108**) is a globular cluster in the southern constellation of [Musca](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Musca/). It is southwest of γ Muscae (Gamma Muscae) and west of the southern end of the Dark Doodad Nebula (Sandqvist 149), a 3° thin streak of black across a southern section of the great plane of the [Milky Way](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Milky_Way/).
