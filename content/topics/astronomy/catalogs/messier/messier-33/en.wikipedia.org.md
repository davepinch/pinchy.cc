  The **Triangulum Galaxy** is a spiral galaxy 2.73 million light-years (ly) from [Earth](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Earth/) in the constellation [Triangulum](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Triangulum/). It is catalogued as **Messier 33** or **NGC (New General Catalogue) 598**. With the D25 isophotal diameter of 18.74 kiloparsecs (61,100 light-years), the Triangulum Galaxy is the third-largest member of the [Local Group](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Local_Group/) of galaxies, behind the [Andromeda Galaxy](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Andromeda_Galaxy/) and the [Milky Way](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Milky_Way/).
