  **Messier 4** or **M4** (also known as **NGC 6121** or the **Spider Globular Cluster**) is a globular cluster in the constellation of [Scorpius](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scorpius/). It was discovered by Philippe Loys de Chéseaux in 1745 and catalogued by Charles Messier in 1764. It was the first globular cluster in which individual stars were resolved.
