  **Messier 79** (also known as **M79** or **NGC 1904**) is a globular cluster in the southern constellation [Lepus](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lepus_(constellation)/). It was discovered by Pierre Méchain in 1780 and is about 42,000 light-years away from [Earth](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Earth/) and 60,000 light years from the [Galactic Center](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Galactic_Center/).
