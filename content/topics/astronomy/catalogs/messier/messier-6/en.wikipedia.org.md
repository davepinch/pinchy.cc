  The **Butterfly Cluster** (cataloged as **Messier 6** or **M6**, and as **NGC 6405**) is an open cluster of stars in the southern constellation of [Scorpius](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scorpius/). Its name derives from the resemblance of its shape to a butterfly.
