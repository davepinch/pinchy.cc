  **Messier 103** (also known as **M103**, or **NGC 581**) is a small open cluster of many faint stars in [Cassiopeia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cassiopeia_(constellation)/). It was discovered on 27 March 1781 by Pierre Méchain, but later added as Charles Messier's last deep-sky object in his catalogue.
