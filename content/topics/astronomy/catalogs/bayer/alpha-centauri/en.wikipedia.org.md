  **Alpha Centauri** (**α Centauri**, **Alpha Cen**, or **α Cen**) is a triple [star system](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Star_system/) in the southern constellation of [Centaurus](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Centaurus/). It consists of three stars: Rigil Kentaurus (Alpha Centauri A), Toliman (B) and [Proxima Centauri](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Proxima_Centauri/) (C). Proxima Centauri is the closest star to the [Sun](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sun/) at 4.2465 light-years (1.3020 pc).
