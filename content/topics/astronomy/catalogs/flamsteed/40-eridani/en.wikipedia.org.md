  **40 Eridani** is a triple star system in the constellation of Eridanus, abbreviated **40 Eri**. It has the Bayer designation **Omicron<sup>2</sup> Eridani**, which is Latinized from **ο<sup>2</sup> Eridani** and abbreviated **Omicron<sup>2</sup> Eri** or **ο<sup>2</sup> Eri**. Based on parallax measurements taken by the Gaia mission, it is about 16.3 light-years from the Sun.
