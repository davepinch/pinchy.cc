  **Alnitak** is a triple [star system](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Star_system/) in the constellation of [Orion](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Orion_(constellation)/). It has the designations **ζ Orionis**, which is Latinised to **Zeta Orionis** and abbreviated **Zeta Ori** or **ζ Ori**, and **50 Orionis**, abbreviated **50 Ori**. The system is located at a distance of several hundred parsecs from the [Sun](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sun/) and is one of the three main stars of Orion's Belt along with [Alnilam](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alnilam/) and [Mintaka](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mintaka/).
