  **Epsilon Virginis** (**ε Virginis**, abbreviated **Epsilon Vir**, **ε Vir**), formally named **Vindemiatrix** /vɪndiːmiˈeɪtrɪks/, is a star in the zodiac constellation of Virgo. The apparent visual magnitude of this star is +2.8, making it the third-brightest member of [Virgo](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Virgo_(constellation)/). Based upon parallax measurements made by the Gaia spapecraft, Vindemiatrix lies at a distance of about 108 light-years (33 parsecs) from the [Sun](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sun/), give or take 0.7 light-years.
