  **Rigel** is a [blue supergiant](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Blue_supergiant/) star in the constellation of [Orion](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Orion_(constellation)/). It has the Bayer designation **β Orionis**, which is Latinized to **Beta Orionis** and abbreviated **Beta Ori** or **β Ori**. Rigel is the brightest and most massive component – and the eponym – of a star system of at least four stars that appear as a single blue-white point of light to the naked eye. This system is located at a distance of approximately 860 light-years (260 pc) from the Sun.
