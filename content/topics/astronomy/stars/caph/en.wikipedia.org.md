  **Beta Cassiopeiae** (**β Cassiopeiae**, abbreviated **Beta Cas** or **β Cas**), officially named **Caph** /ˈkæf/, is a Delta Scuti variable star in the constellation of [Cassiopeia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cassiopeia_(constellation)/). It is a [giant star](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Giant_star/) belonging to the spectral class F2. The white star of second magnitude (+2.28 mag, variable) has an absolute magnitude of +1.3 mag.
