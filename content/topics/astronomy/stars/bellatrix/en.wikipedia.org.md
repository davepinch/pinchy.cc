  **Bellatrix** is the third-brightest star in the constellation of [Orion](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Orion_(constellation)/), positioned 5° west of the red supergiant [Betelgeuse](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Betelgeuse/) (Alpha Orionis). It has the Bayer designation **γ Orionis**, which is Latinized to **Gamma Orionis**. With a slightly variable magnitude of around 1.6, it is typically the 25th-brightest star in the night sky. Located at a distance of 250±10 light-years from the Sun, it is a [blue giant](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Blue_giant/) star around 7.7 times as massive as the sun with 5.75 times its diameter.
