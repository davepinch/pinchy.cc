  **Epsilon Geminorum** or **ε Geminorum**, formally named **Mebsuta** /mɛbˈsuːtə/, is a star in the constellation of [Gemini](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gemini_(constellation)/), on the outstretched right 'leg' of the twin Castor. The apparent visual magnitude of +3.06 makes it one of the brighter stars in this constellation. The distance to this star is determined at 860 light-years (260 parsecs).
