  Due to the precession of [Earth](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Earth/)'s rotational axis, [Thuban](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thuban/) was the naked-eye star closest to the north pole from 3942 BC, when it superseded Tau Herculis as the pole star, until 1793 BC, when it was superseded by Kappa Draconis.
