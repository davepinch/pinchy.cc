  **Alpha Coronae Borealis** (**α Coronae Borealis**, abbreviated **Alpha CrB**, **α CrB**), officially named **Alphecca** /ælˈfɛkə/, is an eclipsing binary star in the constellation of [Corona Borealis](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Corona_Borealis/). It is located about 75 light years from the [Sun](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sun/) and contains two main sequence stars, one class A and one class G.
