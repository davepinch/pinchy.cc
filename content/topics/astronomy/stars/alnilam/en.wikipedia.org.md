  **Alnilam** is the central star of Orion's Belt in the equatorial constellation of [Orion](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Orion_(constellation)/). It has the Bayer designation ε Orionis, which is Latinised to Epsilon Orionis and abbreviated Epsilon Ori or ε Ori. This is a massive, [blue supergiant](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Blue_supergiant/) star some 2,000 light-years distant. It is estimated to be 832,000 times as luminous as the Sun, and 64.5 times as massive.
