  **Orion's Belt** or the **Belt of Orion**, also known as the **Three Kings** or **Three Sisters**, is an asterism in the constellation Orion. It consists of three bright stars – [Alnitak](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alnitak/), Alnilam and Mintaka – which are blue supergiant stars with fainter companions.
