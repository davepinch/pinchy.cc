  **NGC 6025** (also known as **Caldwell 95**) is an open cluster located 2,700 light years away in the [Triangulum Australe](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Triangulum_Australe/) constellation. It was discovered by Abbe Lacaille in 1751 during his expedition to the Cape of Good Hope.
