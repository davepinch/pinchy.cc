  **Alioth** /ˈæliɒθ/, also called **Epsilon Ursae Majoris**, is a star in the northern constellation of [Ursa Major](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ursa_Major/). The designation is Latinised from **ε Ursae Majoris** and abbreviated **Epsilon UMa** or **ε UMa**. Despite being designated "ε" (epsilon), it is the brightest star in the constellation and at magnitude 1.77 is the thirty-third brightest star in the sky.
