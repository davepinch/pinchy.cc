  **Lambda Draconis** (**λ Draconis**, abbreviated **Lam Dra**, **λ Dra**), also named Giausar (/ˈdʒɔːzɑːr/ JAW-zar), is a solitary, orange-red star in the northern circumpolar constellation of [Draco](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Draco_(constellation)/). It is visible to the naked eye with an apparent visual magnitude of +3.85. Based upon an annual parallax shift of 9.79 mas as seen from the [Earth](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Earth/), the star is located around 333 light years from the [Sun](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sun/).
