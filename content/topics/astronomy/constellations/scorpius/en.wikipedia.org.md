  **Scorpius** is a [zodiac](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zodiac/) constellation located in the [Southern celestial hemisphere](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Southern_celestial_hemisphere/), where it sits near the center of the [Milky Way](Milky Way (en.wikipedia.org)), between [Libra](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Libra_(constellation)/) to the west and [Sagittarius](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sagittarius_(constellation)/) to the east.
