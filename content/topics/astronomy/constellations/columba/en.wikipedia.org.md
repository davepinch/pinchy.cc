  **Columba** is a faint constellation designated in the late sixteenth century, remaining in official use, with its rigid limits set in the 20th century. Its name is Latin for dove. It takes up 1.31% of the [southern celestial hemisphere](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Southern_celestial_hemisphere/) and is just south of [Canis Major](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Canis_Major/) and [Lepus](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lepus_(constellation)/).
