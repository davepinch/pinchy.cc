  A **blue supergiant** (**BSG**) is a hot, luminous star, often referred to as an OB [supergiant](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Supergiant/). They are usually considered to be those with luminosity class I and spectral class B9 or earlier, although sometimes A-class supergiants are also deemed blue supergiants.
