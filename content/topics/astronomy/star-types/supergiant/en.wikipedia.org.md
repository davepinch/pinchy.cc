  **Supergiants** are among the most massive and most luminous stars. **Supergiant stars** occupy the top region of the [Hertzsprung–Russell](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hertzsprung–Russell_diagram/) diagram with absolute visual magnitudes between about −3 and −8. The temperature range of supergiant stars spans from about 3,400 K to over 20,000 K.
