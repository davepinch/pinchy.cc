  **Sasquatch! Music Festival** was an annual music festival held at [The Gorge Amphitheatre](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Gorge_Amphitheatre/) in George, [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/), [United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States/). It took place on Memorial Day weekend, running for three to four days. In 2018, it was announced that the festival was canceled indefinitely and would not return in 2019.
