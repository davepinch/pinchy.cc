  **To Your Scattered Bodies Go** (1971) is a science fiction novel by American writer [Philip José Farmer](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philip_José_Farmer/), the first book in the Riverworld series. It won a Hugo Award for Best Novel in 1972 at the 30th Worldcon. The title is derived from the 7th of the "Holy Sonnets" by English poet John Donne.
