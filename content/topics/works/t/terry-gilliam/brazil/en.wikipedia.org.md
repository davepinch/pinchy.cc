  **Brazil** is a 1985 science-fiction dystopian black comedy film directed by [Terry Gilliam](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Terry_Gilliam/) and written by Gilliam, Charles McKeown, and Tom Stoppard. The film stars Jonathan Pryce and features Robert De Niro, Kim Greist, Michael Palin, Katherine Helmond, Bob Hoskins, and Ian Holm.
