  **The Hobbit, or There and Back Again** is a children's fantasy novel by the English author [J. R. R. Tolkien](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/J._R._R._Tolkien/). It was published in 1937 to wide critical acclaim, being nominated for the Carnegie Medal and awarded a prize from the New York Herald Tribune for best juvenile fiction. The book is recognized as a classic in children's literature and is one of the best-selling books of all time, with over 100 million copies sold.
