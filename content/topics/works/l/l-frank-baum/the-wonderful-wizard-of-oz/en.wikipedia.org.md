  ***The Wonderful Wizard of Oz*** is a 1900 children's novel written by author [L. Frank Baum](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/L._Frank_Baum/) and illustrated by W. W. Denslow. It is the first novel in the Oz series of books. A Kansas farm girl named Dorothy ends up in the magical Land of Oz after she and her pet dog Toto are swept away from their home by a cyclone. Upon her arrival in the magical world of Oz, she learns she cannot return home until she has destroyed the Wicked Witch of the West.
