  ***The Hidden Fortress*** (Japanese: 隠し砦の三悪人, Hepburn: *Kakushi Toride no San Akunin*, lit. 'The Three Villains of the Hidden Fortress') is a 1958 Japanese *jidaigeki* adventure film directed by [Akira Kurosawa](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Akira_Kurosawa/), with special effects by Eiji Tsuburaya. It tells the story of two peasants who agree to escort a man and a woman across enemy lines in return for gold without knowing that he is a general and the woman is a princess. The film stars Toshiro Mifune as General Makabe Rokurōta and Misa Uehara as Princess Yuki while the peasants, Tahei and Matashichi, are portrayed by Minoru Chiaki and Kamatari Fujiwara respectively.
