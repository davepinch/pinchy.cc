  The **Quran**, also romanized **Qur'an** or **Koran**, is the central religious text of [Islam](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Islam/), believed by Muslims to be a revelation from God. It is organized in 114 chapters ([surah](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Surah/)) which consist of individual verses (ayat). Besides its religious significance, it is widely regarded as the finest work in Arabic literature, and has significantly influenced the Arabic language. It is also the object of a modern field of academic research known as Quranic studies.
