  **Islam** (/ˈɪslɑːm/; Arabic: ۘالِإسلَام, *al-ʾIslām* [ɪsˈlaːm], transl. "Submission [to God]") is an Abrahamic monotheistic religion centered on the Quran and the teachings of Muhammad.
