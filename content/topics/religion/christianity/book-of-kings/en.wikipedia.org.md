  The **Book of Kings** (Hebrew: סֵפֶר מְלָכִים, *Sēfer Məlāḵīm*) is a book in the Hebrew Bible, found as two books (1–2 Kings) in the [Old Testament](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Old_Testament/) of the Christian Bible. It concludes the Deuteronomistic history, a history of ancient Israel also including the books of Joshua, Judges, and Samuel.
