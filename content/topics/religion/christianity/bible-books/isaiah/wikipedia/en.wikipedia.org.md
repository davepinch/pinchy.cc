  The **Book of Isaiah** (Hebrew: ספר ישעיהו ) is the first of the Latter Prophets in the Hebrew Bible and the first of the major prophets in the Christian Old Testament. It is identified by a superscription as the words of the 8th-century BCE prophet Isaiah, but there is evidence that much of it was composed during the Babylonian captivity and later.
