title: "What is a trespass offering / guilt offering? (gotquestions.org)"
excerpt: >-
  The trespass/guilt offering was required when a person unintentionally violated some of the Lord’s holy things. “Holy things” would normally refer to things that had been dedicated to the Lord—anything from the sanctuary itself to the portion of the offerings that were normally reserved for the priests.
