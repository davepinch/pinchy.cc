  The **Puget Group** is a geologic group in [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/) (state). It preserves fossils dating back to the [Paleogene](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paleogene/) period. {{<pilcrow>}} Two key formations include the [Renton Formation](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Renton_Formation/) and the [Tukwila Formation](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tukwila_Formation/).
