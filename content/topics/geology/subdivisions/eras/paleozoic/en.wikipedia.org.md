  The **Paleozoic** (IPA: /ˌpæli.əˈzoʊ.ɪk,-i.oʊ-, ˌpeɪ-/ *PAL-ee-ə-ZOH-ik*, *-⁠ee-oh-*, *PAY-*; or **Palaeozoic**) Era is the first of three geological eras of the [Phanerozoic](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Phanerozoic/) Eon. Beginning 538.8 million years ago (Ma), it succeeds the [Neoproterozoic](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Neoproterozoic/) (the last era of the Proterozoic Eon) and ends 251.9 Ma at the start of the [Mesozoic](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mesozoic/) Era.
