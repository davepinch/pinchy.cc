  The **Priabonian** is, in the ICS's geologic timescale, the latest age or the upper stage of the [Eocene](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eocene/) Epoch or Series. It spans the time between 37.71 and 33.9 Ma. The Priabonian is preceded by the [Bartonian](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bartonian/) and is followed by the Rupelian, the lowest stage of the Oligocene.
