  The **Bartonian** is, in the International Commission on Stratigraphy's (ICS) geologic time scale, a stage or age in the middle of the [Eocene](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eocene/) Epoch or Series. The Bartonian Age spans the time between 41.2 and 37.71 Ma. It is preceded by the [Lutetian](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lutetian/) and is followed by the Priabonian Age.
