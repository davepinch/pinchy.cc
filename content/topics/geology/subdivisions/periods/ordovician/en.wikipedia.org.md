  The **Ordovician** (/ɔːrdəˈvɪʃi.ən, -doʊ-, -ˈvɪʃən/ or-də-VISH-ee-ən, -⁠doh-, -⁠VISH-ən) is a geologic period and system, the second of six periods of the [Paleozoic](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paleozoic/) Era. The Ordovician spans 41.6 million years from the end of the [Cambrian](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cambrian) Period 485.4 million years ago (Mya) to the start of the [Silurian](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Silurian/) Period 443.8 Mya.
