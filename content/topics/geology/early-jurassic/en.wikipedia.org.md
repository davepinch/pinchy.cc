  The **Early Jurassic** Epoch (in chronostratigraphy corresponding to the **Lower Jurassic** Series) is the earliest of three epochs of the Jurassic Period. The Early Jurassic starts immediately after the Triassic–Jurassic extinction event, 201.3 Ma (million years ago), and ends at the start of the [Middle Jurassic](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Middle_Jurassic/) 174.7 ±0.8 Ma.
