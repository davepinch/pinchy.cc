  The **North American Plate** is a tectonic plate covering most of [North America](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/North_America/), Cuba, the Bahamas, extreme northeastern Asia, and parts of Iceland and the Azores. With an area of 76 million km2 (29 million sq mi), it is the [Earth](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Earth/)'s second largest tectonic plate, behind the Pacific Plate (which borders the plate to the west).
