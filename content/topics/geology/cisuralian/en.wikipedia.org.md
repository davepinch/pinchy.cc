  The **Cisuralian** is the first series/epoch of the [Permian](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Permian/). The Cisuralian was preceded by the [Pennsylvanian](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pennsylvanian_(geology)/) and followed by the [Guadalupian](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Guadalupian/). The Cisuralian Epoch is named after the western slopes of the Ural Mountains in [Russia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russia/) and Kazakhstan and dates between 298.9 ± 0.15 – 272.3 ± 0.5 Ma.
