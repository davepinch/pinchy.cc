  The **Neoproterozoic Era** is the unit of geologic time from 1 billion to 538.8 million years ago. It is the last era of the Precambrian Supereon and the [Proterozoic](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Proterozoic/) Eon; it is subdivided into the [Tonian](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tonian/), [Cryogenian](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cryogenian), and [Ediacaran](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ediacaran/) periods. It is preceded by the [Mesoproterozoic](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mesoproterozoic/) Era and succeeded by the [Paleozoic](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paleozoic/) Era of the [Phanerozoic](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Phanerozoic/) Eon.
