  **Flint**, occasionally **flintstone**, is a sedimentary cryptocrystalline form of the mineral [quartz](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quartz/), categorized as the variety of [chert](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chert/) that occurs in chalk or marly limestone. Historically, flint was widely used to make stone tools and start fires.
