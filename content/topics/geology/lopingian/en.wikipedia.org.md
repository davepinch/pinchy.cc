  The **Lopingian** is the uppermost series/last epoch of the [Permian](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Permian/). It is the last epoch of the [Paleozoic](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paleozoic/). The Lopingian was preceded by the [Guadalupian](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Guadalupian/) and followed by the [Early Triassic](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Early_Triassic/). The Lopingian is often synonymous with the informal terms late Permian or upper Permian.
