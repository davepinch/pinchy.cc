  ***Lawrence of Arabia*** is a 1962 epic biographical adventure drama film based on the life of T. E. Lawrence and his 1926 book *Seven Pillars of Wisdom* (also known as *Revolt in the Desert*). It was directed by David Lean and produced by Sam Spiegel through his British company Horizon Pictures and distributed by Columbia Pictures. The film stars Peter O'Toole as Lawrence with [Alec Guinness](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alec_Guinness/) playing Prince Faisal. The film also stars Jack Hawkins, Anthony Quinn, Omar Sharif, Anthony Quayle, Claude Rains and Arthur Kennedy. The screenplay was written by Robert Bolt and Michael Wilson.
