  **Nineteen Eighty-Four**, also known as **1984**, is a 1984 dystopian drama film written and directed by Michael Radford, based upon [George Orwell](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nineteen_Eighty-Four/)'s 1949 novel of the same name. Starring John Hurt, Richard Burton, Suzanna Hamilton, and Cyril Cusack, the film follows the life of Winston Smith (Hurt), a low-ranking civil servant in a war-torn London ruled by Oceania, a totalitarian superstate. Smith struggles to maintain his sanity and his grip on reality as the regime's overwhelming power and influence persecutes individualism and individual thinking on both a political and personal level.
