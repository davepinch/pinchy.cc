  **TikTok**, whose mainland Chinese counterpart is **Douyin** (Chinese: 抖音; pinyin: *Dǒuyīn*; lit. 'Shaking sound'), is a short-form video hosting service owned by ByteDance. It hosts user-submitted videos, which can range in duration from 3 seconds to 10 minutes.
