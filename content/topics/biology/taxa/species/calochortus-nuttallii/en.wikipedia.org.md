  **Calochortus nuttallii**, also known as the **sego lily**, is a bulbous perennial plant that is endemic to the [Western United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Western_United_States/). The common name of sego comes from a similar Shoshone word. It is the state flower of [Utah](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Utah/).
