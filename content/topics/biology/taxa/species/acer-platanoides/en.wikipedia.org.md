  ***Acer platanoides***, commonly known as the Norway maple, is a species of maple native to eastern and central Europe and western Asia, from [Spain](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spain/) east to [Russia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russia/), north to southern Scandinavia and southeast to northern [Iran](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iran/). It was introduced to North America in the mid-1700s as a shade tree. It is a member of the family Sapindaceae.
