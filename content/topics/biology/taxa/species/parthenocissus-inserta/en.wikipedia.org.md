  ***Parthenocissus inserta*** (syn. *Parthenocissus vitacea*), also known as **thicket creeper**, **false Virginia creeper**, **woodbine**, or **grape woodbine**, is a woody vine native to [North America](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/North_America/), in southeastern [Canada](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Canada/) (west to southern Manitoba) and a large area of the [United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States/), from [Maine](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maine/) west to [Montana](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Montana/) and south to [New Jersey](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_Jersey/) and [Missouri](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Missouri/) in the east, and [Texas](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Texas/) to [Arizona](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arizona/) in the west. It is present in [California](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/California/), but it may be an introduced species that far west. It is introduced in [Europe](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Europe/).
