  ***Salix sitchensis*** is a species of willow known by the common name Sitka willow. It is native to northwestern [North America](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/North_America) from [Alaska](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alaska) to northern [California](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/California) to [Montana](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Montana). It is a common to abundant plant in many types of coastal and inland wetland habitat, such as marshes, riverbanks, swamps, coastal sand dunes, and mountain springs.
