* Categorized as an invasive species.
* This plant was mentioned by the owner of the RV we rented in July 2023. His property extended to the [Skykomish River](/skykomish-river/) in his backyard. While showing us, he mentioned the state was coming out to remove Japanese knotweed, which he described as a bad plant. *July 30, 2023*.
