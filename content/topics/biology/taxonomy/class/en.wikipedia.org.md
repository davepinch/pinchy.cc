  In biological classification, class (Latin: *classis*) is a [taxonomic rank](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Taxonomic_rank/), as well as a taxonomic unit, a taxon, in that rank. It is a group of related taxonomic orders. Other well-known ranks in descending order of size are life, [domain](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Domain_(biology)/), kingdom, [phylum](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Phylum/), order, [family](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Family_(biology)/), genus, and [species](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Species/), with class ranking between phylum and order.
