  A **compass**, more accurately known as a **pair of compasses**, is a technical drawing instrument that can be used for inscribing [circles](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Circle/) or arcs. As dividers, it can also be used as a tool to mark out distances, in particular, on maps. Compasses can be used for mathematics, drafting, navigation and other purposes.
