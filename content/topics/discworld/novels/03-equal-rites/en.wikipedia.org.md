  ***Equal Rites*** is a comic fantasy novel by [Terry Pratchett](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Terry_Pratchett/). Published in 1987, it is the third novel in the *[Discworld](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Discworld/)* series and the first in which the main character is not [Rincewind](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rincewind/). The title is wordplay on the phrase "Equal Rights".
