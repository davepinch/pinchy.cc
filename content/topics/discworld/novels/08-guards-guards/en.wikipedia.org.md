  ***Guards! Guards!*** is a fantasy novel by British writer [Terry Pratchett](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Terry_Pratchett/), the eighth in the *[Discworld](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Discworld/)* series, first published in 1989. It is the first novel about the Ankh-Morpork City Watch. The first *Discworld* point-and-click adventure game borrowed heavily from the plot of Guards! Guards!
