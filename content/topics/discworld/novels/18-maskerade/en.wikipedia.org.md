  ***Maskerade*** is a fantasy novel by British writer [Terry Pratchett](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Terry_Pratchett/), the eighteenth book in the [Discworld](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Discworld/) series. The witches [Granny Weatherwax](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Granny_Weatherwax/) and Nanny Ogg visit the Ankh-Morpork Opera House to find Agnes Nitt, a girl from Lancre, and get caught up in a story similar to The Phantom of the Opera.
