  The **president of the Russian Federation** (Russian: Президент Российской Федерации, tr. *Prezident Rossiyskoy Federatsii*) is the executive head of state of [Russia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russia/). The president is the chair of the Federal State Council and the supreme commander-in-chief of the Russian Armed Forces. It is the highest office in Russia.
