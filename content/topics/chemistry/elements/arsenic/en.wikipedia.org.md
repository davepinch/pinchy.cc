  **Arsenic** is a [chemical element](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chemical_element/) with the symbol **As** and atomic number 33. Arsenic occurs in many minerals, usually in combination with sulfur and metals, but also as a pure elemental crystal. Arsenic is a metalloid. It has various allotropes, but only the grey form, which has a metallic appearance, is important to industry.
