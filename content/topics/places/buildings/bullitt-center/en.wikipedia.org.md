  The **Bullitt** Center is a commercial office building at the intersection of the [Central District](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Central_District,_Seattle/) neighborhood, and [Capitol Hill](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Capitol_Hill,_Seattle/), [Seattle](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seattle/), [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/). It was officially opened on Earth Day, April 22, 2013. The Bullitt Center was designed to be the greenest commercial building in the world, and was certified as a "Living Building" by the International Living Future Institute in April 2015.
