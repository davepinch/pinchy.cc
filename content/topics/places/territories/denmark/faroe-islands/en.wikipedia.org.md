  The **Faroe** or **Faeroe Islands** (/ˈfɛəroʊ/ *FAIR-oh*), or simply the **Faroes** (Faroese: *Føroyar*, pronounced [ˈfœɹjaɹ]; Danish: *Færøerne* [ˈfeɐ̯ˌøˀɐnə]), are an archipelago in the North [Atlantic Ocean](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Atlantic_Ocean/) and an autonomous territory of the Kingdom of Denmark. The official language of the country is Faroese, which is closely related to and partially mutually intelligible with Icelandic.
