title: "Wells Creek Road"
description: >-
  This road is also known as Forest Road 33. It provides access to Nooksack Falls from Washington State Route 542 (Mount Baker Highway). After Nooksack Falls, it crosses the North Fork Nooksack River and follows along Wells Creek. Near the confluence of Wells Creek and Bar Creek, the road crosses Wells Creek and follows Bar Creek for a short distance, before crossing Bar Creek and heading deeper into the forest. The trailhead for Cougar Divide Trail is at the end of the road.
