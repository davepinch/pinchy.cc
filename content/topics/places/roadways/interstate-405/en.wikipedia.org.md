  **Interstate 405** (**I-405**) is a northâ€“south auxiliary Interstate Highway serving the [Seattle](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seattle/) region of [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/), [United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States/). It bypasses Seattle east of [Lake Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lake_Washington/), traveling through the Eastside area of [King](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/King_County,_Washington/) and [Snohomish](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Snohomish_County,_Washington/) counties, providing an alternate route to [I-5](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Interstate_5_in_Washington/). The 30-mile (48 km) freeway serves the cities of [Renton](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Renton,_Washington/), [Bellevue](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bellevue,_Washington/), [Kirkland](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kirkland,_Washington/), and [Bothell](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bothell,_Washington/). I-405 terminates at I-5 in [Tukwila](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tukwila,_Washington/) and [Lynnwood](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lynnwood,_Washington/), and also intersects several major highways, including SR 167, [I-90](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Interstate_90/), [SR 520](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_State_Route_520/), and [SR 522](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_State_Route_522/).
