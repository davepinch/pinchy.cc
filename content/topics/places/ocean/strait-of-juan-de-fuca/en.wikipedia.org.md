> The **Strait of Juan de Fuca** (officially named Juan de Fuca Strait in [Canada](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Canada)) is a body of water about 96 miles (83 nmi; 154 km) long that is the Salish Sea's main outlet to the [Pacific Ocean](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pacific_Ocean). The international boundary between Canada and the [United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States) runs down the centre of the Strait.
