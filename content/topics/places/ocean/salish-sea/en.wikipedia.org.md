  The **Salish Sea** (/ˈseɪlɪʃ/ SAY-lish) is a marginal sea of the [Pacific Ocean](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pacific_Ocean) located in the Canadian province of [British Columbia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/British_Columbia) and the U.S. state of [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)). It includes the Strait of Georgia, the [Strait of Juan de Fuca](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Strait_of_Juan_de_Fuca/), [Puget Sound](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Puget_Sound), and an intricate network of connecting channels and adjoining waterways.
