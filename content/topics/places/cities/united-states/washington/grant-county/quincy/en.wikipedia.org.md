  **Quincy** is a city in [Grant County](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Grant_County,_Washington/), [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/), [United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States/). It is situated east of the [Columbia River](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Columbia_River/) on State Route 28 and north of [Interstate 90](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Interstate_90/). The population was 7,543 at the 2020 census. Quincy is one of the closest cities to [The Gorge Amphitheatre](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Gorge_Amphitheatre/).
