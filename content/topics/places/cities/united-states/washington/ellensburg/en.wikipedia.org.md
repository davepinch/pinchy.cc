  **Ellensburg** is a city in and the county seat of [Kittitas County](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kittitas_County,_Washington/), [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/), [United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States/). It is located just east of the [Cascade Range](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cascade_Range/) near the junction of [Interstate 90](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Interstate_90/) and Interstate 82. The population was 18,666 at the 2020 census. and was estimated to be 19,596 in 2021.
