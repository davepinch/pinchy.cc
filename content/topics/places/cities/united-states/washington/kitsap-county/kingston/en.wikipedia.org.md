  **Kingston** (formerly Appletree Cove) is an unincorporated community and census-designated place (CDP) in [Kitsap County](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kitsap_County,_Washington/), [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/), [United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States/). The population was 2,515 at the 2020 census. Kingston is along the shores of Appletree Cove and [Puget Sound](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Puget_Sound/), and is home to a major Washington State Ferry terminal linking it to Edmonds.
