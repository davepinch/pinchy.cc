  **Port Townsend** /ˈtaʊnzənd/ is a city on the Quimper Peninsula in [Jefferson County](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jefferson_County,_Washington/), [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/), [United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States/). The population was 10,148 at the 2020 United States Census. It is the county seat and only incorporated city of Jefferson County.
