  **Lake Forest Park** is a suburban city in [King County](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/King_County,_Washington), [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)), [United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States), located northeast of [Seattle](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seattle/). It was developed in the 20th century as a bedroom community with single-family housing on medium to large-sized lots. Less than 4% of the city's land is zoned commercial, largely concentrated in one location, and there are no industrial areas.
