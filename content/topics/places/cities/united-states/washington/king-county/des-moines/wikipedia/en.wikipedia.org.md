  **Des Moines** (/dəˈmɔɪnz/ *də-MOYNZ*) is a city in [King County](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/King_County,_Washington/), [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/), [United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States/). The population was 32,888 as of the 2020 census. The city is located on the east shore of [Puget Sound](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Puget_Sound/), near the center of the [Seattle](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seattle/) metropolitan area. It is bordered by the suburbs of [Federal Way](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Federal_Way,_Washington/) to the south, [Kent](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kent,_Washington/) to the east, [SeaTac](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/SeaTac,_Washington/) to the northeast, [Burien](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Burien,_Washington/) to the north, and [Normandy Park](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Normandy_Park,_Washington/) to the northwest.
