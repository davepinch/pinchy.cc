  **Renton** is a city in [King County](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/King_County,_Washington/), [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/), and an inner-ring suburb of [Seattle](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seattle/). Situated 11 miles (18 km) southeast of downtown Seattle, Renton straddles the southeast shore of [Lake Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lake_Washington/), at the mouth of the Cedar River. As of the 2020 census, the population of Renton was 106,785, up from 90,927 at the 2010 census. The city is currently the sixth-largest municipality in greater Seattle and the ninth-largest in Washington state.
