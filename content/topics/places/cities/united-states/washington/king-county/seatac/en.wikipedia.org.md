  **SeaTac** /ˈsiːtæk/ is a city in southern [King County](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/King_County,_Washington), [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)), [United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States/). The city is an inner-ring suburb of [Seattle](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seattle/) and part of the Seattle metropolitan area. The name "SeaTac" is derived from the Seattle–Tacoma International Airport, itself a portmanteau of Seattle and [Tacoma](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tacoma,_Washington/).
