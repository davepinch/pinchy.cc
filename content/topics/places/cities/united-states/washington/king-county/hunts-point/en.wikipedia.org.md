  **Hunts Point** is a town in the Eastside, a region of [King County](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/King_County,_Washington/), [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/), [United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States/), and part of the [Seattle](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seattle/) metropolitan area. The town is on a small peninsula surrounded by [Lake Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lake_Washington/), and is near the suburbs of [Medina](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Medina,_Washington/) (to the southwest), [Clyde Hill](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Clyde_Hill,_Washington/) (to the south), [Yarrow Point](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yarrow_Point,_Washington/) (on another peninsula to the east), and [Kirkland](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kirkland,_Washington/) (to the northeast), as well as the city of [Bellevue](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bellevue,_Washington/) (to the east). As of the 2010 census, the town had a total population of 394.
