---
title: "Brier may not technically border Lynnwood"
related:
  - Brier, Washington
  - Lynnwood, Washington
---
According to Wikipedia, Brier borders Lynnwood. According to Bing Maps, the boundaries of Brier and Lynnwood do not touch. However, Alderwood Manor does border Lynnwood and Wikipedia notes that some addresses in Alderwood Manor can use Brier as an address.
