  **Sultan** is a city in [Snohomish County](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Snohomish_County,_Washington/), [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/), [United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States/).
