  On June 28, 1905, the town of [Sultan](/sultan/) in [Snohomish County](/snohomish-county/) is officially incorporated as a town of the fourth class. The voters of the small logging community, located at the confluence of the [Sultan](/sultan-river/) and [Skykomish](/skykomish-river/) rivers a few miles east of [Monroe](/monroe/), unanimously approved incorporation on June 10, 1905, and also elected their first mayor, Hartford M. Meredith (1840-1907), and other town officials.
