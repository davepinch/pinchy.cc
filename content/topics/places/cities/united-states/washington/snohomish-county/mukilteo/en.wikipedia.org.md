  **Mukilteo** (/ˌmʌkəlˈtiːoʊ/ MUK-əl-TEE-oh; Lushootseed: bək̓ʷəɬtiwʔ) is a city in [Snohomish County](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Snohomish_County,_Washington/), [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/), [United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States/). It is located on Puget Sound between [Edmonds](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Edmonds,_Washington/) and [Everett](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Everett,_Washington/), approximately 25 miles (40 km) north of [Seattle](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seattle/). The city had a population of 20,254 at the 2010 census and an estimated 2019 population of 21,441.
