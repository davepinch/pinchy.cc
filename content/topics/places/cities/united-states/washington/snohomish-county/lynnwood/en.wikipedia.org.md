  **Lynnwood** is a city in [Snohomish County](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Snohomish_County,_Washington/), [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/), [United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States/). The city is part of the Seattle metropolitan area and is located 16 miles (26 km) north of [Seattle](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seattle/) and 13 miles (21 km) south of [Everett](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Everett,_Washington/), near the junction of [Interstate 5](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Interstate_5_in_Washington/) and Interstate 405. It is the fourth-largest city in Snohomish County, with a population of 38,568 in the 2020 U.S. census.
