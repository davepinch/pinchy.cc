  **Snohomish** is a city in [Snohomish County](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Snohomish_County,_Washington/), [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/), [United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States/). The population was 10,126 at the 2020 census. It is located on the [Snohomish River](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Snohomish_River/), southeast of [Everett](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Everett,_Washington/) and northwest of [Monroe](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Monroe,_Washington/). Snohomish lies at the intersection of U.S. Route 2 and State Route 9. The city's airport, Harvey Airfield, is located south of downtown and used primarily for general aviation.
