  **Martha Lake** is a census-designated place (CDP) in [Snohomish County](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Snohomish_County,_Washington/), [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/), [United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States/). The population was 21,129 at the 2020 census. It lies west of [Mill Creek](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mill_Creek,_Washington/) and northeast of [Lynnwood](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lynnwood,_Washington/), near the lake of the same name.
