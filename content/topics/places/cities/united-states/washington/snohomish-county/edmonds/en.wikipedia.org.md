  **Edmonds** is a city in [Snohomish County](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Snohomish_County,_Washington/), [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/), [United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States/). It is located in the southwest corner of the county, facing [Puget Sound](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Puget_Sound/) and the [Olympic Mountains](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Olympic_Mountains/) to the west. The city is part of the Seattle metropolitan area and is located 15 miles (24 km) north of [Seattle](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seattle/) and 18 miles (29 km) southwest of [Everett](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Everett,_Washington/). With a population of 42,853 residents in the 2020 U.S. census, Edmonds is the third most populous city in the county.
