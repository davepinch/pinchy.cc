  **Esperance** is a census-designated place (CDP) in [Snohomish County](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Snohomish_County,_Washington/), [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/), [United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States/). Esperance is an enclave of the city of [Edmonds, Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Edmonds,_Washington/). The population was 4,007 at the 2020 census, up from 3,601 at the 2010 census.
