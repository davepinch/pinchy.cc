  **Sequim** (/ˈskwɪm/ *SKWIM*) is a city in [Clallam County](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Clallam_County,_Washington/), [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/), [United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States/). It is located along the Dungeness River near the base of the Olympic Mountains. The 2020 census counted a population of 8,018, with an estimated population in 2021 of 8,241.
