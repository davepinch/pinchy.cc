**Sultan** is a city in [Snohomish County](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Snohomish_County,_Washington), [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)), [United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States). It is located approximately 23 miles (37 km) east of Everett at the confluence of the [Skykomish River](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skykomish_River) and the Sultan River, a minor tributary. The city had a population of 4,651 at the 2010 census.
