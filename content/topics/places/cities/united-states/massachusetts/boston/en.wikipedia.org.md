  **Boston** (US: /ˈbɔːstən/), officially the **City of Boston**, is the capital and most populous city of the U.S. state of [Massachusetts](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Massachusetts/), and the cultural and financial center of [New England](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_England/) in the [Northeastern United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Northeastern_United_States/), with an area of 48.4 sq mi (125 km2) and a population of 675,647 in 2020. Greater Boston metropolitan statistical area is the eleventh-largest in the country.
