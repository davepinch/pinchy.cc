  **Dayton** (/ˈdeɪtən/) is a city in and the county seat of Montgomery County, [Ohio](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ohio/), [United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States). A small part of the city extends into Greene County. As of the 2020 census, the city proper had a population of 137,644, making it the sixth-most populous city in Ohio. Dayton anchors the state's fourth-largest metropolitan area, the Dayton metropolitan area, which had 814,049 residents. Dayton is located within Ohio's Miami Valley region, 50 miles (80 km) north of [Cincinnati](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cincinnati/) and 60 miles (97 km) west of Columbus. It is a principal city of the Dayton–Springfield–Sidney combined statistical area, home to a population of 1,086,512.
