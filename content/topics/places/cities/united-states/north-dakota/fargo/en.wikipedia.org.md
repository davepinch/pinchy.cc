  **Fargo** is a city in and the county seat of Cass County, [North Dakota](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/North_Dakota), [United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States). According to the 2020 census, its population was 125,990, making it the most populous city in the state and the 219th-most populous city in the United States.
