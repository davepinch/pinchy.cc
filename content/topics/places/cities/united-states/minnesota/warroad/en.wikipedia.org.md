> **Warroad** is a city in Roseau County, [Minnesota](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Minnesota), [United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States), at the southwest corner of Lake of the Woods, 7.5 miles (12.1 km) south of [Canada](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Canada). The population was 1,781 at the 2010 census. Warroad had its own newspaper before it was incorporated in 1901.
