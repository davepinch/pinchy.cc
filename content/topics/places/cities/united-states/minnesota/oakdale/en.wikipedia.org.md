  **Oakdale** is a city in [Washington County](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_County,_Minnesota/), [Minnesota](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Minnesota/), [United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States/).
