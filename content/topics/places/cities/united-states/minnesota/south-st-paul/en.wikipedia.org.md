  **South St. Paul** is a city in [Dakota County](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dakota_County,_Minnesota/), [Minnesota](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Minnesota/), [United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States/), immediately south and southeast of [St. Paul](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Saint_Paul,_Minnesota/) and east of [West St. Paul](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/West_St._Paul,_Minnesota/). The population was 20,759 at the 2020 census. The town was a major meat-packing location, and many residents are descended from immigrants of Southern European and Eastern European heritage, who came to work in the meat-packing plants in the early twentieth century.
