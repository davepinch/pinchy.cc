  **Mahtomedi** (/ˌmɑːtoʊˈmiːdaɪ/ MAH-toh-MEE-dy) is a city in [Washington County](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_County,_Minnesota/), [Minnesota](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Minnesota/), [United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States/). The population was 7,676 at the 2010 census. Mahtomedi is a suburb of St. Paul and is located between St. Paul and Stillwater.
