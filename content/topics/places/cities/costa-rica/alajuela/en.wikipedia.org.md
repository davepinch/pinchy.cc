  **Alajuela** (Spanish pronunciation: [alaËˆxwela]) is a district in the Alajuela canton of the Alajuela Province of [Costa Rica](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Costa_Rica/). As the seat of the Municipality of Alajuela canton, it is awarded the status of city. By virtue of being the city of the first canton of the province, it is also the capital of the Province of Alajuela.
