  **Tokyo** (/ˈtoʊkioʊ/; Japanese: 東京, *Tōkyō*, [toːkʲoː]), officially the **Tokyo Metropolis** (東京都, *Tōkyō-to*), is the capital of [Japan](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Japan/) and the most populous city in the world with a population of over 14 million residents as of 2023. The Tokyo metropolitan area, which includes Tokyo and nearby prefectures, is the world's most-populous metropolitan area with 40.8 million residents as of 2023.
