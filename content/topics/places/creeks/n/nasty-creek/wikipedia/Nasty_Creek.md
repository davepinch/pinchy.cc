  **Nasty Creek** is a stream located in the upper reach of the Ahtanum Creek watershed, in Yakima County, Washington, in the United States. The stream flows 3.7 miles (6.0 km) from its source near Foundation Ridge to its confluence with the North Fork of Ahtanum Creek.
