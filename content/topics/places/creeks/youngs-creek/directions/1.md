title: "Start on Ben Howard Rd"
details: Ben Howard Road is on the south side of the Skykomish River and can be accessed from either Monroe (heading east) or Sultan (heading west). In both cases, you will cross the Skykomish and continue in the same direction on Ben Howard.
next: "Turn south onto Cedar Ponds Rd."
monroe, washington: Take S. Lewis St (highway 203) across the river and turn left onto Ben Howard Rd. You will then be traveling east.
sultan, washington: Take Mann Rd. across the river and turn right onto Ben Howard Rd. You will then be traveling west.
