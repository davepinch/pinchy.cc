---
title: "Access Youngs Creek Falls at the second bridge on Cedar Point Road."
details:
  - There is parking in both directions from the bridge.
  - Do not block the blue gate near the bridge (or any blue gate anywhere).
  - To access upstream of the waterfall, take the path next to the bridge.
  - To access downstream, to the grassy parking spot uphill from the bridge (where you came from) and find the trail behind the grass.
  - Otherwise keep driving to the next bridge for another access point.
