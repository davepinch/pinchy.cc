---
title: "Access Marckworth Falls at the third bridge on Cedar Point Road when coming from the west"
access of: Marckworth Falls
details:
  - Marckworth Falls is under the second of three bridges when driving on Cedar Point Road.
  - You will see a blue gate to the right, uphill from the bridge.
  - There are a few parking spots next to the gate.
  - Remember to never block the blue gates or the road.
  - To access the creek, find the path near the parking spot.
