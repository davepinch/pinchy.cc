  The **Sammamish River Trail** is a 10.1-mile (16.3 km) bike path and recreational rail trail in [King County](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/King_County,_Washington), [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)) that runs along the [Sammamish River](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sammamish_River/) from Blyth Park in [Bothell]([Bothell](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bothell,_Washington)) to [Marymoor Park](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marymoor_Park/) in [Redmond](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Redmond,_Washington/) as part of the “Locks to Lakes Corridor.” It connects to the Burke-Gilman Trail at its northwestern end, and to the Redmond Central Connector at its southeastern end.
