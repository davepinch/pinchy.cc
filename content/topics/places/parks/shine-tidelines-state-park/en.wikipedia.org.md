  **Shine Tidelands State Park** is a 249-acre (101 ha) [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/) state park located in [Jefferson County](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jefferson_County,_Washington/), seven miles (11 km) south of Port Ludlow. The park has 5,000 feet (1,500 m) of shoreline on Bywater Bay adjacent to the west end of the [Hood Canal Bridge](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hood_Canal_Bridge/) and offers activities including picnicking, fishing, shellfish harvesting, beachcombing, birdwatching, windsurfing, and wildlife viewing. In 2014, the park was expanded to include the former Wolfe and Point Hannon properties.
