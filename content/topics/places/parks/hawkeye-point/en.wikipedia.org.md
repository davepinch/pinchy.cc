  **Hawkeye Point** is the highest natural point in [Iowa](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iowa/) at 1,670 feet (510 m). It is approximately 4.5 miles (7.2 km) north of Sibley on the eastern side of SR 60 and approximately 3.5 miles (5.6 km) south of the Iowa-Minnesota state border. The high ground lies 100 feet (30 m) due south of an old silo.
