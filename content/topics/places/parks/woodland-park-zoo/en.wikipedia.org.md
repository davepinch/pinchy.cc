  **Woodland Park Zoo** is a wildlife conservation organization and zoological garden located in the [Phinney Ridge](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Phinney_Ridge,_Seattle/) neighborhood of [Seattle](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seattle/), [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/), [United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States/). The zoo is the recipient of over 65 awards across multiple categories. The zoo has around 900 animals from 250 species and the zoo has over 1 million visitors a year.
