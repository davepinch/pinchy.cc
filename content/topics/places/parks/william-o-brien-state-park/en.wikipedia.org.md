  **William O'Brien State Park** is a 1,520-acre (6.2 km2) state park of [Minnesota](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Minnesota/), USA, along the [St. Croix River](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/St._Croix_River_(Wisconsin–Minnesota)/). Its hiking trails traverse rolling glacial moraine, riparian zones, restored oak savanna, wooded areas and bogs. It is a popular place for birdwatching, picnics, camping, cross-country skiing, canoeing, fishing, and other typical outdoor recreational activities.
