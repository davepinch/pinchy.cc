  **Washington Park** is a public park in [Seattle](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seattle/), [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/), [United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States/), most of which is taken up by the Washington Park Arboretum, a joint project of the University of Washington, the Seattle Parks and Recreation, and the nonprofit Arboretum Foundation. Washington Park also includes a playfield and the Seattle Japanese Garden in its southwest corner. To the north is [Union Bay](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Union_Bay_(Seattle)/); to the west are [Montlake](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Montlake,_Seattle/) and Madison Valley; to the south is the Washington Park neighborhood; and to the east is the Broadmoor Golf Club.
