  The **Manus Plate** is a 100-km microplate located northeast of New Guinea. The Manus Plate was formed in between the North Bismark Plate and the [South Bismark Plate](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/South_Bismarck_Plate/). The Manus Plate currently rotates counter-clockwise in the Melanesia area.
