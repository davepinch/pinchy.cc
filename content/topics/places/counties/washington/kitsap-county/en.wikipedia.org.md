  **Kitsap County** is located in the U.S. state of [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/). As of the 2020 census, its population was 275,611. Its county seat is Port Orchard, and its largest city is [Bremerton](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bremerton,_Washington/). The county was formed out of [King County](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/King_County,_Washington/) and [Jefferson County](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jefferson_County,_Washington/) on January 16, 1857, and is named for Chief Kitsap of the Suquamish Tribe. Originally named Slaughter County, it was soon renamed.
