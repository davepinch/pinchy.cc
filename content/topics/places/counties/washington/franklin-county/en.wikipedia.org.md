  **Franklin County** is a county located in the U.S. state of [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/). As of the 2020 census, its population was 96,749. The county seat and largest city is Pasco. The county was formed out of [Whitman County](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Whitman_County,_Washington/) on November 28, 1883, and is named for Benjamin Franklin.
