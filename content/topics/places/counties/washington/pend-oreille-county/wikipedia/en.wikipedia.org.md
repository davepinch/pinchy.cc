  **Pend Oreille County** (/ˌpɒndəˈreɪ/ POND-ə-RAY) is a county located in the northeast corner of the U.S. state of [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Okanogan_County,_Washington/), along the Canada–US border. As of the 2020 census, the population was 13,401. The county seat and largest city is Newport.
