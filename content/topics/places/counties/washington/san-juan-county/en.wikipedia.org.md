  **San Juan County** (Spanish: Condado de San Juan) is a county located in the Salish Sea in the far northwestern corner of the U.S. state of [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/). As of the 2020 census, its population was 17,788. The county seat and only incorporated city is Friday Harbor, located on San Juan Island.
