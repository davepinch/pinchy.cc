  **Columbia County** is a county located in the U.S. state of [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/). As of the 2020 census, the population was 3,952, making it the second-least populous county in Washington. The county seat and largest city is Dayton. The county was created out of [Walla Walla County](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Walla_Walla_County,_Washington/) on November 11, 1875, and is named after the [Columbia River](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Columbia_River/) (which does not flow through the county). Columbia County is included in the Walla Walla, WA Metropolitan Statistical Area.
