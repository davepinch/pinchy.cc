  **Grant County** is a county located in the U.S. state of [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/). As of the 2020 census, the population was 99,123. The county seat is Ephrata, and the largest city is Moses Lake. The county was formed out of [Douglas County](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Douglas_County,_Washington/) in February 1909 and is named for U.S. President Ulysses S. Grant.
