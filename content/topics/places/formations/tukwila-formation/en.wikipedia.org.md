  The **Tukwila Formation** is a geological formation in [King County](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/King_County,_Washington/), [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/) within the [Puget Group](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Puget_Group/). It is named after Tukwila area, which is close to the formation. The formation consists of various fossils of marine origin.
