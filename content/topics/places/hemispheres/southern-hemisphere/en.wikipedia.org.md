excerpt: >-
  The **Southern Hemisphere** is the half (hemisphere) of [Earth](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Earth/) that is south of the Equator. It contains all or parts of five continents (the whole of [Antarctica](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antarctica/), the whole of [Australia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Australia_(continent)/), about 90% of [South America](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/South_America/), about one-third of [Africa](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Africa/), and some islands off the continental mainland of [Asia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Asia/)) and four oceans (the whole Southern Ocean, the majority of the Indian Ocean, the South Atlantic Ocean, and the South Pacific Ocean), as well as New Zealand and most of the Pacific Islands in Oceania. Its surface is 80.9% water, compared with 60.7% water in the [Northern Hemisphere](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Northern_Hemisphere/), and it contains 32.7% of Earth's land.
