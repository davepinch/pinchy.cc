  **Lake Itasca** (/aɪˈtæskə/ eye-TASS-kə) is a small glacial lake, approximately 1.8 square miles (4.7 km2; 1,200 acres) in area. Located in southeastern Clearwater County, in the Headwaters area of north central [Minnesota](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Minnesota/), it is notable for being the headwater of the [Mississippi River](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mississippi_River/). The lake is in Itasca State Park. It has an average depth of 20 to 35 feet (6.1 to 10.7 m) and is 1,475 feet (450 m) above sea level.
