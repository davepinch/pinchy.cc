  **Lake Ballinger** is a freshwater lake with a surface area of 103 acres in southern [Snohomish County](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Snohomish_County,_Washington/), [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/). It is bordered by the cities of [Mountlake Terrace](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mountlake_Terrace,_Washington/) to the east and [Edmonds](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Edmonds,_Washington/) to the west. It is fed by Hall Creek at its north end and its outflow is McAleer Creek at its east end, going on to feed [Lake Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lake_Washington/).
