  The **Montlake Cut** is the easternmost section of the Lake Washington Ship Canal, which passes through the city of [Seattle](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seattle/), linking [Lake Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lake_Washington/) to [Puget Sound](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Puget_Sound/). It opened in 1916 after 56 years of conversation and construction to create the manmade canal.
