  The **Gare de Lyon**, officially **Paris-Gare-de-Lyon**, is one of the seven large mainline railway stations in [Paris](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paris/), [France](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/France/). It handles about 148.1 million passengers annually according to the estimates of the SNCF in 2018, with SNCF railways and the RER D accounting for around 110 million and the RER A accounting for 38 million, making it the second-busiest station of France after the Gare du Nord and one of the busiest in Europe.
