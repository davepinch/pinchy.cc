  The **Fenelon Place Elevator** (also known as the **Fourth Street Elevator**) is a 3 ft (914 mm) narrow gauge funicular railway located in [Dubuque](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dubuque,_Iowa/), [Iowa](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iowa/), [United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States/). It was listed in the National Register of Historic Places in 1978. It was included as a contributing property in the Cathedral Historic District in 1985, and in the Fenelon Place Residential Historic District in 2015.
