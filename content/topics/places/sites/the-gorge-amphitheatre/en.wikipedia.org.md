  **The Gorge Amphitheatre**, originally known as Champs de Brionne Music Theatre, is an outdoor concert venue in [Grant County](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Grant_County,_Washington/), [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/), [United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States/). It is situated near [Columbia River](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Columbia_River/) in Central Washington, nine miles (14 km) west of George. The venue is managed by Live Nation.
