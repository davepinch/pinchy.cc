  **Central Asia** is a subregion of [Asia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Asia/) that stretches from the [Caspian Sea](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Caspian_Sea/) in the southwest and Eastern Europe in the northwest to Western China and [Mongolia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mongolia/) in the east, and from [Afghanistan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Afghanistan) and [Iran](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iran/) in the south to [Russia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russia/) in the north. It includes [Kazakhstan](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kazakhstan/), [Kyrgyzstan](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kyrgyzstan/), [Tajikistan](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tajikistan/), [Turkmenistan](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Turkmenistan/), and [Uzbekistan](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Uzbekistan/). The countries as a group are also colloquially referred to as the "-stans" as all have names ending with the Persian suffix "-stan" (meaning "land of") in both respective native languages and most other languages.
