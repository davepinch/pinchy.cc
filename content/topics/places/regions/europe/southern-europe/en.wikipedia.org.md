  **Southern Europe** is the southern region of [Europe](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Europe/). It is also known as Mediterranean Europe, as its geography is marked by the [Mediterranean Sea](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mediterranean_Sea/). Definitions of southern Europe include some or all of these countries and regions: Albania, Andorra, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Bulgaria, Croatia, Cyprus, Gibraltar, Greece, [Italy](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Italy/), [Malta](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Malta/), Monaco, Montenegro, North Macedonia, Portugal, San Marino, Serbia, Slovenia, southern France, Spain, Turkey (East Thrace), and Vatican City.
