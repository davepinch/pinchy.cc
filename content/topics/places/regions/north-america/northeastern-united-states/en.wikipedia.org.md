  The **Northeastern United States**, also referred to as the **Northeast**, the **East Coast**, or the **American Northeast**, is a geographic region of the [United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States/) located on the Atlantic coast of [North America](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/North_America/). It borders [Canada](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Canada/) to its north, the Southern United States to its south, the [Midwestern United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Midwestern_United_States/) to its west, and the [Atlantic Ocean](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Atlantic_Ocean/) to its east.
