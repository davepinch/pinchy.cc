  **Snoqualmie Pass** is a mountain pass that carries [Interstate 90](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Interstate_90/) (I-90) through the [Cascade Range](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cascade_Range/) in the U.S. state of [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/). The pass summit is at an elevation of 3,015 feet (919 m), on the county line between [Kittitas County](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kittitas_County,_Washington) and [King County](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/King_County,_Washington/).
