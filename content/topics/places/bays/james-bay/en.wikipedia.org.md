  **James Bay** (French: *Baie James*; Cree: ᐐᓂᐯᒄ, romanized: *Wînipekw*, lit. 'dirty water') is a large body of water located on the southern end of [Hudson Bay](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hudson_Bay/) in [Canada](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Canada/). It borders the provinces of [Quebec](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quebec/) and [Ontario](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ontario/), and is politically part of [Nunavut](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nunavut/). Its largest island is Akimiski Island.
