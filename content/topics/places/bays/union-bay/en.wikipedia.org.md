  **Union Bay** is a body of water located in [Seattle](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seattle/), [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/). Part of [Lake Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lake_Washington/), it is bounded by the [Laurelhurst](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Laurelhurst,_Seattle/) neighborhood to the north and the [Montlake](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Montlake,_Seattle/) and [Madison Park](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Madison_Park,_Seattle/) neighborhoods to the south. The Evergreen Point Floating Bridge, which carries [State Route 520](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_State_Route_520/), crosses over a portion of the bay.
