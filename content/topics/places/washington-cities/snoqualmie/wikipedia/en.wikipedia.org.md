  **Snoqualmie** (/snoʊˈkwɔːlmi/ *snow-KWAWL-mee*) is a city next to Snoqualmie Falls in King County, Washington, United States. It is 28 miles (45 km) east of Seattle. Snoqualmie is home to the Northwest Railway Museum. The population was 14,121 at the 2020 census.
