  **Columbia City** is a neighborhood located in the southeastern part of [Seattle](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seattle/), [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/) in the [Rainier Valley](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rainier_Valley,_Seattle/) district. It has a landmark-protected historic business district and is one of the few Seattle neighborhoods with a long history of ethnic and income diversity.
