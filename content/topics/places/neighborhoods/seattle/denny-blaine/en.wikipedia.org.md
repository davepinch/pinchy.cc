  **Denny-Blaine** (also known as **Harrison**) is a neighborhood in east central [Seattle](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seattle/), [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/). It is bounded on the east by [Lake Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lake_Washington/); on the south by E. Howell Street, beyond which is [Madrona](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Madrona,_Seattle/); on the west by 32nd Avenue, beyond which is Madison Valley; and on the north by Lake Washington Boulevard E., Hillside Drive E., and E. Prospect Street, beyond which are Washington Park and [Madison Park](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Madison_Park,_Seattle/).
