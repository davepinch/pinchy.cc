  **Licton Springs** or **North College Park** is a neighborhood in the informal Northgate district of North Seattle. It is bounded by [Interstate 5](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Interstate_5_in_Washington/) to the east, beyond which is [Maple Leaf](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maple_Leaf,_Seattle/) neighborhood and the Northgate Mall; Aurora Avenue N (SR 99) to the west, beyond which is [Greenwood](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Greenwood,_Seattle/); N 85th Street to the south, beyond which is [Green Lake](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Green_Lake,_Seattle/), and N Northgate Way to the north, beyond which is [Haller Lake](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Haller_Lake,_Seattle/).
