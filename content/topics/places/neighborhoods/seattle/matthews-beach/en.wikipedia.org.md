  **Matthews Beach** is a neighborhood in [Seattle](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seattle/), [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/); it and [Meadowbrook](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Meadowbrook,_Seattle/) are the southern neighborhoods of the annexed township of [Lake City](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lake_City,_Seattle/) (1954). Matthews Beach lies about 2 miles (3.2 km) northeast of the University of Washington, about 8 miles (13 km) northeast of Downtown.
