  **Georgetown** is a neighborhood in [Seattle](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seattle/), [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/), [United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States/). It is bounded on the north by the mainlines of the BNSF Railway and Union Pacific Railroad, beyond which is the Industrial District; on the west by the Duwamish River, across which is South Park; on the east by [Interstate 5](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Interstate_5_in_Washington/), beyond which is [Beacon Hill](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Beacon_Hill,_Seattle/); and on the south by Boeing Field.
