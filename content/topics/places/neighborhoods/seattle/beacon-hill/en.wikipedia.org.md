  **Beacon Hill** is a hill and neighborhood in southeast [Seattle](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seattle/), [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/). It is roughly bounded on the west by [Interstate 5](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Interstate_5_in_Washington/), on the north by [Interstate 90](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Interstate_90/), on the east by Rainier Avenue South, Cheasty Boulevard South, and Martin Luther King Junior Way South, and on the south by the Seattle city boundary. It is part of Seattle's South End.
