  **SoDo**, alternatively **SODO**, is a neighborhood in [Seattle](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seattle/), [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/), that makes up part of the city's Industrial District. It is bounded on the north by South King Street, beyond which is [Pioneer Square](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pioneer_Square,_Seattle/); on the south by South Spokane Street, beyond which is more of the Industrial District; on the west by the Duwamish River, across which is [West Seattle](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/West_Seattle/); and on the east by Metro Transit's Downtown Seattle Transit Tunnel and SoDo Busway, beyond which is the International District and the rest of the Industrial District.
