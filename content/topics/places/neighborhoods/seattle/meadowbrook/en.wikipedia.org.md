  **Meadowbrook** is a neighborhood in the [Lake City](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lake_City,_Seattle/) district (township annexed in 1954) of [Seattle](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seattle/), [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/). Meadowbrook is centered on open fields adjacent to the Community Center, Meadowbrook swimming pool, and Nathan Hale High School. It is bounded on the south by NE 95th Street and the [Wedgwood](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wedgwood,_Seattle/) neighborhood, on the north by NE 120th Street and Cedar Park, on the west by Lake City Way NE (State Route 522) and Victory Heights, and on the east by 35th Avenue NE and Matthews Beach.
