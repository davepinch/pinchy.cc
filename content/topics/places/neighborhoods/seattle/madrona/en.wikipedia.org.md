  **Madrona** is a mostly residential neighborhood in east [Seattle](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seattle), [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)). It is bounded on the east by [Lake Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lake_Washington/); on the south by E. Cherry Street, beyond which is Leschi; on the west by Martin Luther King Jr. Way, beyond which is the [Central District](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Central_District,_Seattle/); and on the north by E. Howell Street, beyond which is Denny-Blaine.
