  **Ravenna** is a neighborhood in northeastern [Seattle](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seattle/), [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/) named after Ravenna, [Italy](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Italy/). Though Ravenna is considered a residential neighborhood, it also is home to several businesses, many of which are located in the University Village, a shopping mall.
