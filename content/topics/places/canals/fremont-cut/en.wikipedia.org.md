  The **Fremont Cut** is a canal in [Seattle](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seattle/), [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/), [United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States/), that connects [Lake Union](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lake_Union/) to the east with [Salmon Bay](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Salmon_Bay/) to the west. It is part of the [Lake Washington Ship Canal](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lake_Washington_Ship_Canal/) linking [Lake Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lake_Washington/) to [Puget Sound](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Puget_Sound/), and is 5,800 feet (1,800 m) long and 270 feet (82 m) wide. The center channel is 100 feet (30 m) wide and 30 feet (9.1 m) deep.
