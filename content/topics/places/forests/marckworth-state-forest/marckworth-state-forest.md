title: "Marckworth State Forest"
description: >-
  The Marckworth State Forest is [state trust land](https://www.dnr.wa.gov/managed-lands/forest-and-trust-lands) that is managed by the Washington Department of Natural Resources. The forest is open for recreation but has no facilities or official trails. You can drive and walk along logging roads. There are several beautiful waterfalls and viewpoints.
