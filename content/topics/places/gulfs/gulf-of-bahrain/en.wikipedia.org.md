  The **Gulf of Bahrain** is an inlet of the [Persian Gulf](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Persian_Gulf/) on the east coast of [Saudi Arabia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Saudi_Arabia/), separated from the main body of water by the peninsula of [Qatar](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Qatar/). It surrounds the islands of [Bahrain](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bahrain/). The King Fahd Causeway crosses the western section of the Gulf of Bahrain, connecting Saudi Arabia to Bahrain.
