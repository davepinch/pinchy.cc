  **Hurricane Ridge** is a mountainous area in Washington's [Olympic National Park](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Olympic_National_Park/). Approximately 18 miles (29 km) by road from [Port Angeles](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Port_Angeles,_Washington/), the ridge is open to hiking, skiing, and snowboarding and is one of the two most visited sites in the national park (along with the Hoh Rainforest).
