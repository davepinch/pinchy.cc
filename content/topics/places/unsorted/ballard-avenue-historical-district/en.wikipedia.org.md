  The **Ballard Avenue Historic District** is a section of downtown [Ballard](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ballard,_Seattle/) in [Seattle](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seattle/), [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/), that was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1976 (ID #76001885). The district consists of Ballard Avenue N.W. between N.W. Market Street and N.W. Dock Place, and is located near to and along [Salmon Bay](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Salmon_Bay/).
