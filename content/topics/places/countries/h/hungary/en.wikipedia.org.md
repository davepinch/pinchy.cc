  **Hungary** is a landlocked country in [Central Europe](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Central_Europe/). Spanning 93,030 square kilometres (35,920 sq mi) of the Carpathian Basin, it is bordered by [Slovakia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Slovakia/) to the north, [Ukraine](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ukraine/) to the northeast, [Romania](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Romania/) to the east and southeast, [Serbia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Serbia/) to the south, [Croatia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Croatia/) and [Slovenia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Slovenia/) to the southwest, and [Austria](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Austria/) to the west. Hungary has a population of 9.6 million, mostly ethnic Hungarians and a significant Romani minority. Hungarian is the official language, and Budapest is the country's capital and largest city.
