  **Honduras**, officially the **Republic of Honduras**, is a country in [Central America](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Central_America/). Honduras is bordered to the west by [Guatemala](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Guatemala/), to the southwest by [El Salvador](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/El_Salvador/), to the southeast by [Nicaragua](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nicaragua/), to the south by the [Pacific Ocean](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pacific_Ocean/) at the Gulf of Fonseca, and to the north by the Gulf of Honduras, a large inlet of the [Caribbean Sea](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Caribbean_Sea/). Its capital and largest city is Tegucigalpa.
