  **Tanzania**, officially the **United Republic of Tanzania**, is a country in East Africa within the African Great Lakes region. It is bordered by [Uganda](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Uganda/) to the northwest; [Kenya](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kenya/) to the northeast; the [Indian Ocean](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indian_Ocean/) to the east; [Mozambique](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mozambique/) and [Malawi](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Malawi/) to the south; [Zambia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zambia/) to the southwest; and [Rwanda](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rwanda/), [Burundi](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Burundi/), and the [Democratic Republic of the Congo](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Democratic_Republic_of_the_Congo/) to the west. Mount Kilimanjaro, Africa's highest mountain, is in northeastern Tanzania. According to the 2022 national census, Tanzania has a population of nearly 62 million, making it the most populous country located entirely south of the equator.
