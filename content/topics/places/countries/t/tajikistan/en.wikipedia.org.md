  **Tajikistan**, officially the **Republic of Tajikistan**, is a landlocked country in Central Asia. Dushanbe is the capital and most populous city. Tajikistan is bordered by [Afghanistan](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Afghanistan/) to the south, [Uzbekistan](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Uzbekistan/) to the west, [Kyrgyzstan](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kyrgyzstan/) to the north, and [China](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/China/) to the east. It is separated from [Pakistan](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pakistan/) by Afghanistan's Wakhan Corridor.
