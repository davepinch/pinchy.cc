  **Sri Lanka**, historically known as **Ceylon** and officially the **Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka**, is an island country in [South Asia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/South_Asia/). It lies in the Indian Ocean, southwest of the Bay of Bengal, separated from the Indian peninsula by the Gulf of Mannar and the Palk Strait. It shares a maritime border with the Maldives in the southwest and India in the northwest.
