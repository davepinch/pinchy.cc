  **Switzerland**, officially the **Swiss Confederation**, is a landlocked country located at the confluence of Western, Central and Southern Europe. It is bordered by [Italy](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Italy/) to the south, [France](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/France/) to the west, [Germany](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Germany/) to the north and [Austria](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Austria/) and [Liechtenstein](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Liechtenstein/) to the east.
