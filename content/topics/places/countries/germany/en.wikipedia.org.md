  **Germany**, officially the **Federal Republic of Germany**, is a country in Central Europe. It is the second-most populous country in [Europe](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Europe/) after [Russia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russia/), and the most populous member state of the European Union. Germany is situated between the [Baltic](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Baltic_Sea/) and North seas to the north, and the Alps to the south. Its 16 constituent states are bordered by [Denmark](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Denmark) to the north, [Poland](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Poland/) and the [Czech Republic](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Czech_Republic/) to the east, [Austria](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Austria/) and [Switzerland](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Switzerland/) to the south, and [France](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/France/), [Luxembourg](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Luxembourg/), [Belgium](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Belgium/), and the [Netherlands](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Netherlands/) to the west. The nation's capital and most populous city is [Berlin](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Berlin/) and its main financial centre is Frankfurt; the largest urban area is the Ruhr.
