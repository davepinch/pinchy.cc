  **Iceland** (Icelandic: Ísland, pronounced [ˈistlant]) is a Nordic island country between the North Atlantic and [Arctic](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arctic_Ocean/) Oceans, on the Mid-Atlantic Ridge between [North America](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/North_America/) and [Europe](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Europe/). It is linked culturally and politically with Europe and is the region's most sparsely populated country. Its capital and largest city is Reykjavík, which is home to about 36% of the country's roughly 380,000 residents. The official language of the country is Icelandic.
