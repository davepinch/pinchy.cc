  **Chad**, officially the **Republic of Chad**, is a country located at the crossroads of North and Central Africa. The landlocked country is bordered by [Libya](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Libya/) to the north, Sudan to the east, the Central African Republic to the south, Cameroon to the southwest, Nigeria to the southwest (at Lake Chad), and Niger to the west. Chad has a population of 16 million, of which 1.6 million live in the capital and largest city of N'Djamena. With a total area of around 1,300,000 km2 (500,000 sq mi), Chad is the fifth-largest country in Africa and the twentieth largest nation by area in the world.
