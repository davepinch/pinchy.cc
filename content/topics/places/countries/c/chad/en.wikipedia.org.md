  **Chad**, officially the **Republic of Chad**, is a country located at the crossroads of North and Central Africa. The landlocked country is bordered by [Libya](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Libya/) to the north, [Sudan](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sudan/) to the east, the [Central African Republic](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Central_African_Republic/) to the south, [Cameroon](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cameroon/) to the southwest, [Nigeria](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nigeria/) to the southwest (at Lake Chad), and [Niger](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Niger/) to the west. Chad has a population of 16 million, of which 1.6 million live in the capital and largest city of N'Djamena. With a total area of around 1,300,000 km2 (500,000 sq mi), Chad is the fifth-largest country in Africa and the twentieth largest nation by area in the world.
