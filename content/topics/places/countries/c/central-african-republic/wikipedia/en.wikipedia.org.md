  The **Central African Republic** (**CAR**), formerly known as **Ubangi-Shari**, is a landlocked country in Central Africa. It is bordered by [Chad](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chad/) to the north, [Sudan](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sudan/) to the northeast, [South Sudan](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/South_Sudan/) to the east, the [Democratic Republic of the Congo](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Democratic_Republic_of_the_Congo/) to the south, the [Republic of the Congo](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Republic_of_the_Congo/) to the southwest, and [Cameroon](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cameroon/) to the west. Bangui is the country's capital and largest city, at the border with the Democratic Republic of the Congo. The Central African Republic covers a land area of about 620,000 square kilometres (240,000 sq mi). As of 2021, it had an estimated population of around 5.5 million. As of 2024, the Central African Republic is the scene of a civil war, which has been ongoing since 2012.
