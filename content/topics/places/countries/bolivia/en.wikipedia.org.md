  **Bolivia**, officially the **Plurinational State of Bolivia**, is a landlocked country located in western-central [South America](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/South_America/). It is bordered by [Brazil](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Brazil/) to the north and east, [Paraguay](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paraguay/) to the southeast, [Argentina](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Argentina/) to the south, [Chile](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chile/) to the southwest, and [Peru](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Peru/) to the west. The seat of government and administrative capital is La Paz, which contains the executive, legislative, and electoral branches of government, while the constitutional capital is Sucre, the seat of the judiciary. The largest city and principal industrial center is Santa Cruz de la Sierra, located on the Llanos Orientales (eastern tropical lowlands), a mostly flat region in the east of the country.
