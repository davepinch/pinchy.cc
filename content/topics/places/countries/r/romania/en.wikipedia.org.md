excerpt: >-
  **Romania** is a country at the crossroads of [Central](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Central_Europe/), [Eastern](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eastern_Europe/) and [Southeastern](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Southeast_Europe/) Europe. It borders [Ukraine](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ukraine/) to the north and east, [Hungary](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hungary/) to the west, [Serbia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Serbia/) to the southwest, [Bulgaria](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bulgaria/) to the south, [Moldova](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Moldova/) to the east, and the [Black Sea](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Black_Sea/) to the southeast. It has a predominantly continental climate, and an area of 238,397 km2 (92,046 sq mi) with a population of 19 million people (2023). Romania is the twelfth-largest country in Europe and the sixth-most populous member state of the European Union. Its capital and largest city is Bucharest, followed by Cluj-Napoca, Iași, Timișoara, Constanța, Craiova, Brașov, and Galați.
