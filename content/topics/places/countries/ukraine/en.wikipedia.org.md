  **Ukraine** (/juːˈkreɪn/ yoo-KRAYN; Ukrainian: Україна, romanized: *Ukraina*, pronounced [ʊkrɐˈjinɐ]) is a country in Eastern Europe. It is the second-largest European country after [Russia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russia/), which borders it to the east and northeast. It also borders Belarus to the north; [Poland](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Poland/), Slovakia, and Hungary to the west; and Romania and Moldova to the southwest; with a coastline along the Black Sea and the Sea of Azov to the south and southeast. Kyiv is the nation's capital and largest city, followed by Kharkiv, Dnipro and Odesa. Ukraine's official language is Ukrainian; Russian is also widely spoken, especially in the east and south.
