  **Mali**, officially the **Republic of Mali**, is a landlocked country in West Africa. Mali is the eighth-largest country in [Africa](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Africa/), with an area of over 1,241,238 square kilometres (479,245 sq mi). The country is bordered to the north by [Algeria](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Algeria/), to the east by [Niger](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Niger/), to the northwest by [Mauritania](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mauritania/), to the south by Burkina Faso and Ivory Coast, and to the west by Guinea and Senegal. The population of Mali is 21.9 million, 67% of which was estimated to be under the age of 25 in 2017. Its capital and largest city is Bamako. The country has 13 official languages, of which Bambara is the most commonly spoken.
