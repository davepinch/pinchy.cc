  **Mexico** (Spanish: México), officially the **United Mexican States**, is a country in the southern portion of [North America](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/North_America). It is bordered to the north by the [United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States); to the south and west by the [Pacific Ocean](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pacific_Ocean); to the southeast by [Guatemala](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Guatemala/), Belize, and the [Caribbean Sea](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Caribbean_Sea/); and to the east by the [Gulf of Mexico](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gulf_of_Mexico). Mexico covers 1,972,550 km2 (761,610 sq mi), making it the world's 13th-largest country by area; with a population of over 126 million, it is the 10th-most-populous country and has the most Spanish speakers. Mexico is organized as a federal republic comprising 31 states and Mexico City, its capital. Other major urban areas include Monterrey, Guadalajara, Puebla, Toluca, Tijuana, Ciudad Juárez, and León.
