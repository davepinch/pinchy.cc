  The **Maldives**, officially the **Republic of Maldives**, and historically known as the **Maldive Islands**, is a country and archipelagic state in [South Asia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/South_Asia/) in the Indian Ocean. The Maldives is southwest of Sri Lanka and India, about 750 kilometres (470 miles; 400 nautical miles) from the Asian continent's mainland. The Maldives' chain of 26 atolls stretches across the equator from Ihavandhippolhu Atoll in the north to Addu Atoll in the south.
