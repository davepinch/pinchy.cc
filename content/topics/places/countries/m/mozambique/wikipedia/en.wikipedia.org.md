  **Mozambique**, officially the **Republic of Mozambique**, is a country located in southeast Africa bordered by the [Indian Ocean](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indian_Ocean/) to the east, [Tanzania](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tanzania/) to the north, [Malawi](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Malawi/) and [Zambia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zambia/) to the northwest, [Zimbabwe](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zimbabwe/) to the west, and [Eswatini](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eswatini/) and [South Africa](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/South_Africa/) to the southwest. The sovereign state is separated from the Comoros, Mayotte and Madagascar by the Mozambique Channel to the east. The capital and largest city is Maputo.
