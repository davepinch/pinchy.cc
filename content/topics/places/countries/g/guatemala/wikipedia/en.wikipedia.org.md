  **Guatemala**, officially the **Republic of Guatemala**, is a country in [Central America](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Central_America/). It is bordered to the north and west by [Mexico](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mexico/), to the northeast by Belize, to the east by [Honduras](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Honduras/), and to the southeast by [El Salvador](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/El_Salvador/). It is hydrologically bordered to the south by the [Pacific Ocean](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pacific_Ocean/) and to the northeast by the Gulf of Honduras.
