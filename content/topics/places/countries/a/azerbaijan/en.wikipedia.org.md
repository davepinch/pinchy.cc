  **Azerbaijan**, officially the **Republic of Azerbaijan**, is a transcontinental country located at the boundary of Eastern Europe and [West Asia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/West_Asia/). It is a part of the South Caucasus region and is bounded by the Caspian Sea to the east, [Russia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russia/)'s republic of Dagestan to the north, [Georgia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Georgia_(country)/) to the northwest, [Armenia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Armenia/) and [Turkey](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Turkey/) to the west, and [Iran](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iran/) to the south. Baku is the capital and largest city.
