  **Armenia** (/ɑːrˈmiːniə/  ar-MEE-nee-ə), officially the **Republic of Armenia**, is a landlocked country in the Armenian Highlands of [West Asia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/West_Asia/). It is a part of the Caucasus region and is bordered by [Turkey](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Turkey/) to the west, [Georgia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Georgia_(country)/) to the north and [Azerbaijan](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Azerbaijan/) to the east, and [Iran](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iran/) and the Azerbaijani exclave of [Nakhchivan](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nakhchivan_Autonomous_Republic/) to the south. Yerevan is the capital, largest city and financial center.
