  **Zambia**, officially the **Republic of Zambia**, is a landlocked country at the crossroads of Central, Southern and East Africa. It is typically referred to being in South-Central Africa or Southern Africa. It is bordered to the north by the Democratic Republic of the Congo, [Tanzania](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tanzania/) to the north-east, Malawi to the east, [Mozambique](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mozambique/) to the southeast, [Zimbabwe](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zimbabwe/) and [Botswana](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Botswana/) to the south, Namibia to the southwest, and Angola to the west. The capital city of Zambia is Lusaka, located in the south-central part of Zambia. The population is concentrated mainly around Lusaka in the south and the Copperbelt Province to the north, the core economic hubs of the country.
