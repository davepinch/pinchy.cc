  **Zimbabwe** (/zɪmˈbɑːbweɪ, -wi/; Shona pronunciation: [zi.ᵐba.ɓwe]), officially the **Republic of Zimbabwe**, is a landlocked country in Southern Africa, between the Zambezi and Limpopo Rivers, bordered by [South Africa](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/South_Africa/) to the south, [Botswana](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Botswana/) to the southwest, [Zambia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zambia/) to the north, and [Mozambique](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mozambique/) to the east. The capital and largest city is Harare, and the second largest is Bulawayo.
