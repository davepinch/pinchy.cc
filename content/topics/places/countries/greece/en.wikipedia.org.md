  **Greece**, officially the **Hellenic Republic**, is a country in Southeast Europe, located on the southern tip of the Balkan peninsula. Greece shares land borders with [Albania](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Albania/) to the northwest, [North Macedonia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/North_Macedonia/) and [Bulgaria](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bulgaria/) to the north, and [Turkey](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Turkey/) to the east. The Aegean Sea lies to the east of the mainland, the [Ionian Sea](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ionian_Sea/) to the west, and the Sea of Crete and the [Mediterranean Sea](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mediterranean_Sea/) to the south. Greece has the longest coastline on the Mediterranean Basin, featuring thousands of islands. It has a population of nearly 10.3 million (as of 2024). Athens is the nation's capital and the largest city, followed by Thessaloniki and Patras.
