  **Pakistan**, officially the **Islamic Republic of Pakistan**, is a country in [South Asia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/South_Asia/). It is the fifth-most populous country, with a population of over 241.5 million, having the second-largest Muslim population as of 2023. Islamabad is the nation's capital, while Karachi is its largest city and financial centre. Pakistan is the 33rd-largest country by area and the ninth-largest in Asia. Bounded by the [Arabian Sea](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arabian_Sea/) on the south, the [Gulf of Oman](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gulf_of_Oman/) on the southwest, and the Sir Creek on the southeast, it shares land borders with [India](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/India/) to the east; [Afghanistan](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Afghanistan/) to the west; [Iran](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iran/) to the southwest; and [China](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/China/) to the northeast. It shares a maritime border with Oman in the [Gulf of Oman](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gulf_of_Oman/), and is separated from [Tajikistan](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tajikistan/) in the northwest by Afghanistan's narrow Wakhan Corridor.
