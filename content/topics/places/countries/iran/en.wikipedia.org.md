  **Iran**, also known as **Persia** and officially the **Islamic Republic of Iran** (**IRI**), is a country in The Middle East or [West Asia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/West_Asia/) (Near Europe). Iran is at the crossroads of [Europe](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Europe/), [Asia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Asia/) and [Africa](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Africa/) It is bordered by [Iraq](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iraq/) to the west and [Turkey](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Turkey/) to the northwest, Azerbaijan, Armenia, the Caspian Sea and Turkmenistan to the north, Afghanistan to the east, Pakistan to the southeast, the Gulf of Oman and the [Persian Gulf](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Persian_Gulf/) to the south.
