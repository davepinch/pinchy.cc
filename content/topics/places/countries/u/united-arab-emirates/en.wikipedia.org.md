  The **United Arab Emirates** (**UAE**), or simply the **Emirates**, is a country in [West Asia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/West_Asia/), in the [Middle East](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Middle_East/). Located at the eastern end of the Arabian Peninsula, it shares borders with [Oman](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Oman/) and [Saudi Arabia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Saudi_Arabia/); as well as maritime borders in the [Persian Gulf](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Persian_Gulf/) with [Qatar](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Qatar/) and [Iran](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iran/). The United Arab Emirates is an elective monarchy formed from a federation of seven emirates. As of 2023, the UAE has an estimated population of 9.97 million. Emirati citizens are estimated to form 11.6% of the population; the remaining residents are expatriates, the majority of whom are South Asian. Islam is the official religion and Arabic is the official language. Abu Dhabi is the country's capital, while Dubai, the most populous city, is an international hub.
