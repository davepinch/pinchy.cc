  **India**, officially the **Republic of India** (ISO: *Bhārat Gaṇarājya*), is a country in [South Asia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/South_Asia/). It is the seventh-largest country by area; the most populous country as of June 2023; and from the time of its independence in 1947, the world's most populous democracy. Bounded by the [Indian Ocean](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indian_Ocean/) on the south, the [Arabian Sea](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arabian_Sea/) on the southwest, and the Bay of Bengal on the southeast, it shares land borders with [Pakistan](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pakistan/) to the west; [China](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/China/), Nepal, and Bhutan to the north; and [Bangladesh](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bangladesh/) and Myanmar to the east. In the Indian Ocean, India is in the vicinity of Sri Lanka and the Maldives; its Andaman and Nicobar Islands share a maritime border with Thailand, Myanmar, and Indonesia.
