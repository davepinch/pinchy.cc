  **Kyrgyzstan**, officially the Kyrgyz Republic, is a landlocked country in [Central Asia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Central_Asia/), lying in the Tian Shan and Pamir mountain ranges. Bishkek is the capital and largest city of the country. Kyrgyzstan is bordered by [Kazakhstan](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kazakhstan/) to the north, [Uzbekistan](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Uzbekistan/) to the west, [Tajikistan](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tajikistan/) to the south, and [China](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/China/) to the east and southeast. Ethnic Kyrgyz make up the majority of the country's 7 million people, followed by significant minorities of Uzbeks and Russians.
