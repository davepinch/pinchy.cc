  **Turkmenistan** is a country in Central Asia bordered by [Kazakhstan](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kazakhstan/) to the northwest, [Uzbekistan](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Uzbekistan/) to the north, east and northeast, [Afghanistan](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Afghanistan/) to the southeast, [Iran](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iran/) to the south and southwest and the [Caspian Sea](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Caspian_Sea/) to the west. Ashgabat is the capital and largest city. It is one of the six independent Turkic states. With a population of 6.5 million, Turkmenistan is the 35th most-populous country in Asia and has the lowest population of the Central Asian republics while being one of the most sparsely populated nations on the Asian continent.
