  **Niger** or **the Niger**, officially the **Republic of the Niger**, is a country in West Africa. It is a unitary state bordered by [Libya](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Libya/) to the northeast, [Chad](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chad/) to the east, [Nigeria](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nigeria/) to the south, [Benin](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Benin/) and [Burkina Faso](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Burkina_Faso/) to the southwest, [Mali](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mali/) to the west, and [Algeria](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Algeria/) to the northwest. It covers a land area of almost 1,270,000 km2 (490,000 sq mi), making it the largest landlocked country in West Africa and the second largest landlocked nation in Africa behind Chad. Over 80% of its land area lies in the Sahara. Its predominantly Muslim population of about 25 million lives mostly in clusters in the south and west of the country. The capital Niamey is located in Niger's southwest corner.
