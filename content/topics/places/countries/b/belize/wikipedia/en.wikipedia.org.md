  **Belize** (/bɪˈliːz, bɛ-/, *bih-LEEZ*, *beh-*; Belize Kriol English: *Bileez*) is a country on the north-eastern coast of [Central America](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Central_America/). It is bordered by [Mexico](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mexico/) to the north, the [Caribbean Sea](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Caribbean_Sea/) to the east, and [Guatemala](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Guatemala/) to the west and south. It also shares a water boundary with [Honduras](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Honduras/) to the southeast.
