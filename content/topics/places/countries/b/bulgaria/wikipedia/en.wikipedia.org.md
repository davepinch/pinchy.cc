  **Bulgaria** (/bʌlˈɡɛəriə, bʊl-/; Bulgarian: България, romanized: *Bŭlgariya*), officially the **Republic of Bulgaria**, is a country in [Southeast Europe](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Southeast_Europe/). Located west of the [Black Sea](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Black_Sea/) and south of the Danube river, Bulgaria is bordered by [Greece](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Greece/) and [Turkey](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Turkey/) to the south, [Serbia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Serbia/) and [North Macedonia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/North_Macedonia/) to the west, and [Romania](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Romania/) to the north. It covers a territory of 110,994 square kilometres (42,855 sq mi) and is the 16th largest country in Europe. Sofia is the nation's capital and largest city; other major cities include Burgas, Plovdiv, and Varna.
