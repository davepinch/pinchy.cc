  **Venezuela**, officially the **Bolivarian Republic of Venezuela**, is a country on the northern coast of [South America](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/South_America/), consisting of a continental landmass and many islands and islets in the [Caribbean Sea](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Caribbean_Sea/). Venezuela comprises an area of 916,445 km^2 (353,841 sq mi), and its population was estimated at 29 million in 2022. The capital and largest urban agglomeration is the city of Caracas.
