  **Liechtenstein** (/ˈlɪktənstaɪn/ *LIK-tən-styne*; German: [ˈlɪçtn̩ʃtaɪn]), officially the **Principality of Liechtenstein** (German: Fürstentum Liechtenstein, pronounced [ˈfʏʁstn̩tuːm ˈlɪçtn̩ˌʃtaɪ̯n]), is a landlocked German-speaking microstate located in the Alps between [Austria](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Austria/) and [Switzerland](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Switzerland/).
