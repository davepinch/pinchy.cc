title: "Key Peninsula"
note: >-
  The Key Pensinsula is a peninsula on the south side of the Kitsap Peninsula, itself a peninsula of the larger Olympic Peninsula. Most of the Key Peninsula is within Pierce County, although small chunks are part of Kitsap County and Mason County on the north side.
