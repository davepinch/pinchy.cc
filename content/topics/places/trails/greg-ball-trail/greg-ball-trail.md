title: "Greg Ball Trail"
note: >-
  The south trailhead connects to Railroad Grade and Woody Trail in the Wallace Falls State Park. The trail then runs northward parallel with the North Fork Wallace River. Along the way it runs by Wallace Lake and Jay Lake, where it ends.

  - Snohomish County, Washington
