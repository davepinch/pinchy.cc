  **Manitoba** (/ˌmænɪˈtoʊbə/ MAN-ih-TOH-bə) is a province of [Canada](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Canada/) at the longitudinal centre of the country. It is Canada's fifth-most populous province, with a population of 1,342,153 as of 2021. Manitoba has a widely varied landscape, from arctic tundra and the [Hudson Bay](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hudson_Bay/) coastline in the north to dense boreal forest, large freshwater lakes, and prairie grassland in the central and southern regions.
