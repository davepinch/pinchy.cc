  **Chechnya**, officially the **Chechen Republic**, is a republic of [Russia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russia/). It is situated in the North Caucasus of Eastern Europe, between the [Caspian Sea](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Caspian_Sea/) and [Black Sea](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Black_Sea/). The republic forms a part of the North Caucasian Federal District, and shares land borders with [Georgia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Georgia_(country)/) to its south; with the Russian republics of [Dagestan](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dagestan/), Ingushetia, and North Ossetia-Alania to its east, north, and west; and with [Stavropol Krai](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stavropol_Krai/) to its northwest.
