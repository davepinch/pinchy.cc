  **Dagestan** (/ˌdæɡɪˈstæn, -ˈstɑːn/ DAG-ə-STA(H)N; Russian: Дагестан; IPA: [dəɡʲɪˈstan]), officially the **Republic of Dagestan**, is a republic of [Russia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russia/) situated in the North Caucasus of Eastern Europe, along the [Caspian Sea](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Caspian_Sea/). It is located north of the Greater Caucasus, and is a part of the North Caucasian Federal District. The republic is the southernmost tip of Russia, sharing land borders with the countries of [Azerbaijan](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Azerbaijan/) and [Georgia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Georgia_(country)/) to the south and southwest, the Russian republics of [Chechnya](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chechnya/) and [Kalmykia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kalmykia/) to the west and north, and with [Stavropol Krai](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stavropol_Krai/) to the northwest. Makhachkala is the republic's capital and largest city; other major cities are Derbent, Kizlyar, Izberbash, Kaspiysk, and Buynaksk.
