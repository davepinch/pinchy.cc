  **Kalmykia**, officially the **Republic of Kalmykia**, is a republic of Russia, located in the North Caucasus region of Southern Russia. The republic is part of the Southern Federal District, and borders [Dagestan](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dagestan/) to the south and [Stavropol Krai](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stavropol_Krai/) to the southwest; [Volgograd Oblast](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Volgograd_Oblast/) to the northwest and north and [Astrakhan Oblast](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Astrakhan_Oblast/) to the north and east; [Rostov Oblast](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rostov_Oblast/) to the west and the [Caspian Sea](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Caspian_Sea/) to the east. Through the Caspian Depression, the Kuma river forms Kalmykia's natural border with Dagestan. Kalmykia is the only region in Europe where Buddhism is the predominant religion.
