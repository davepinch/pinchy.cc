  **Indiana** (/ˌɪndiˈænə/ (listen)) is a U.S. state in the [Midwestern United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Midwestern_United_States/). It is the 38th-largest by area and the 17th-most populous of the 50 States. Its capital and largest city is [Indianapolis](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indianapolis/). Indiana was admitted to the United States as the 19th state on December 11, 1816. It is bordered by Lake Michigan to the northwest, [Michigan](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Michigan/) to the north, [Ohio](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ohio/) to the east, the Ohio River and [Kentucky](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kentucky/) to the south and southeast, and the Wabash River and [Illinois](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Illinois/) to the west.
