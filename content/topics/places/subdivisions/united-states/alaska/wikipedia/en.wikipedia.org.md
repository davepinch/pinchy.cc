  **Alaska** (/əˈlæskə/ ə-LAS-kə) is a non-contiguous U.S. state on the northwest extremity of [North America](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/North_America). It borders [British Columbia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/British_Columbia) and Yukon in [Canada](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Canada) to the east and it shares a western maritime border in the Bering Strait with [Russia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russia/)'s Chukotka Autonomous Okrug. The Chukchi and Beaufort Seas of the Arctic Ocean lie to the north and the [Pacific Ocean](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pacific_Ocean) lies to the south. Technically a semi-exclave of the U.S., Alaska is the largest exclave in the world.
