excerpt: >-
  The **Bab-el-Mandeb** (Arabic: باب المندب, lit. 'Gate of Lamentation', Tigrinya: ባብ ኣል ማንዳብ), the **Gate of Grief** or the **Gate of Tears**, is a strait between [Yemen](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yemen/) on the Arabian Peninsula and [Djibouti](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Djibouti/) and [Eritrea](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eritrea/) in the Horn of Africa. It connects the [Red Sea](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Red_Sea/) to the [Gulf of Aden](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gulf_of_Aden/) and by extension the [Indian Ocean](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indian_Ocean/).
