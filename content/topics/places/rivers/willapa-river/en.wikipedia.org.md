  The **Willapa River** is a river on the [Pacific](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pacific_Ocean/) coast of southwestern [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)) in the [United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States), approximately 20 miles (32 km) long. It drains an area of low hills and a coastal plain into Willapa Bay, a large estuary north of the mouth of the [Columbia River](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Columbia_River).
