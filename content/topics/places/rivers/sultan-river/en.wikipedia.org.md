  The **Sultan River** is a river in [Snohomish County](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Snohomish_County,_Washington/) in the U.S. state of [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/). It is a tributary of the [Skykomish River](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skykomish_River/), which it joins at the town of [Sultan](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sultan,_Washington/). The river is dammed in its upper third by Culmback Dam to form Spada Lake.
