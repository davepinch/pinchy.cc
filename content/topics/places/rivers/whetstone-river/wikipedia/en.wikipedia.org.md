  The **Whetstone River** is a 12.7-mile-long (20.4 km) tributary of the [Minnesota River](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Minnesota_River/), in northeastern [South Dakota](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/South_Dakota/) and a very small portion of western [Minnesota](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Minnesota/) in the United States. Via the Minnesota River, it is part of the watershed of the [Mississippi River](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mississippi_River/).
