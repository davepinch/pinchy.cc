  **Vancouver Island** is an island in the northeastern [Pacific Ocean](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pacific_Ocean/) and part of the Canadian province of [British Columbia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/British_Columbia/). The island is 456 km (283 mi) in length, 100 km (62 mi) in width at its widest point, and 32,100 km2 (12,400 sq mi) in total area, while 31,285 km2 (12,079 sq mi) are of land. The island is the largest by area and the most populous along the west coasts of the Americas.
