title: "Guam"
note: >-
  Guam is both an island and a United States territory. The boundaries of each are different, as the territorial boundaries extend around the island into the ocean. Wikidata and Wikipedia do not distinguish between the island and territory. However, they are different--for example, the territory could change but the island would remain the same. The Wiki pages are shared for now (20 April 2026).
