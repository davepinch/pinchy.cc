  The **San Juan Islands** is an archipelago in the [Pacific Northwest](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pacific_Northwest/) of the [United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States/) between the U.S. state of [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/) and [Vancouver Island](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vancouver_Island/), [British Columbia]([British Columbia](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/British_Columbia/)), [Canada](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Canada/). The San Juan Islands are part of Washington state, and form the core of San Juan County.
