  The **Ballard Bridge**, also known as the **15th Avenue Bridge**, is a double-leaf bascule bridge in [Seattle](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seattle/), [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)/). It carries 15th Avenue NW over Seattle's [Salmon Bay](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Salmon_Bay/) between [Ballard](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ballard,_Seattle/) to the north and [Interbay](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Interbay,_Seattle/) to the south. The Ballard Bridge follows the [Fremont Bridge](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fremont_Bridge_(Seattle)/) in the east in the succession of bridges spanning the [Lake Washington Ship Canal](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lake_Washington_Ship_Canal/), which connects [Lake Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lake_Washington/) in the east to [Puget Sound](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Puget_Sound/) in the west.
