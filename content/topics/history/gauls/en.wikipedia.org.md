  The **Gauls** (Latin: Galli; Ancient Greek: Γαλάται, *Galátai*) were a group of Celtic peoples of mainland Europe in the [Iron Age](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iron_Age/) and the Roman period (roughly 5th century BC to 5th century AD). Their homeland was known as Gaul (Gallia). They spoke Gaulish, a continental Celtic language.
