  **Charles J. Kirk** (born October 14, 1993) is an American conservative political activist, radio talk show host, and internet personality. He founded Turning Point USA with Bill Montgomery in 2012, and has served as its executive director since. He is the CEO of Turning Point Action, Turning Point Academy, Turning Point Faith, president of Turning Point Endowment, and a member of the Council for National Policy. Kirk has written four books.
