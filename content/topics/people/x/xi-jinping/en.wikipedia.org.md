  **Xi Jinping** (Chinese: 习近平; pinyin: *Xí Jìnpíng*, pronounced [ɕǐ tɕîn.pʰǐŋ]; born 15 June 1953) is a Chinese politician who has been the general secretary of the [Chinese Communist Party](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chinese_Communist_Party/) (CCP) and chairman of the Central Military Commission (CMC), and thus as the [paramount leader](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paramount_leader/) of China, since 2012. Xi has also been the president of the People's Republic of China (PRC) since 2013. He belongs to the fifth generation of Chinese leadership.
