  **Alejandro Jodorowsky Prullansky** (Spanish: [xoðoˈɾofski]; born 17 February 1929) is a Chilean and French avant-garde filmmaker. Known for his films *El Topo* (1970), *The Holy Mountain* (1973) and *Santa Sangre* (1989), Jodorowsky has been "venerated by cult cinema enthusiasts" for his work which "is filled with violently surreal images and a hybrid blend of mysticism and religious provocation".
