  **Aleister Crowley** (/ˈælɪstər ˈkroʊli/; born **Edward Alexander Crowley**; 12 October 1875 – 1 December 1947) was an English occultist, philosopher, ceremonial magician, poet, painter, novelist and mountaineer. He founded the religion of [Thelema](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thelema/), identifying himself as the prophet entrusted with guiding humanity into the Æon of Horus in the early 20th century. A prolific writer, he published widely over the course of his life.
