  **Barack Hussein Obama II** (born August 4, 1961) is an American politician who served as the 44th [president of the United States](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_the_United_States/) from 2009 to 2017. As a member of the Democratic Party, he was the first African-American president in U.S. history. Obama previously served as a U.S. senator representing Illinois from 2005 to 2008, and as an Illinois state senator from 1997 to 2004.
