  **Pál Szalai** (September 3, 1915 – January 16, 1994) also spelled **Pál Szalay** and later anglicized as **Paul Sterling** was a high-ranking Hungarian police officer and reinstated member of the Arrow Cross Party after 1944. In 1945, together with Swedish diplomat [Raoul Wallenberg](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Raoul_Wallenberg), Szalai helped save hundreds of Hungarian Jews in the Budapest ghetto. In 2009, he was honored by Israel as Righteous among the Nations.
