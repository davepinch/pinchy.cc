  **Pope Francis** (Latin: *Franciscus*; Italian: *Francesco*; Spanish: *Francisco*; born **Jorge Mario Bergoglio**; 17 December 1936) is the head of the Catholic Church, the bishop of Rome and sovereign of the Vatican City State. He is the only pope to be a member of the Society of Jesus (Jesuits), the only one from the Americas, the only one from the Southern Hemisphere, and the only one born or raised outside Europe since the 8th-century papacy of the Syrian Pope Gregory III.
