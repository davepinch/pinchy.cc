  **James Maury Henson** (September 24, 1936 – May 16, 1990) was an American puppeteer, animator, cartoonist, actor, inventor, and filmmaker who achieved worldwide notice as the creator of The Muppets and *Fraggle Rock* (1983–1987) and director of *[The Dark Crystal](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Dark_Crystal)* (1982) and *Labyrinth* (1986). He was born in Greenville, [Mississippi](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mississippi/), and raised in both Leland, Mississippi, and University Park, [Maryland](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maryland/).
