  **Julien Offray de La Mettrie** (French: [ɔfʁɛ də la metʁi]; November 23, 1709 – November 11, 1751) was a French physician and philosopher, and one of the earliest of the French materialists of the Enlightenment. He is best known for his 1747 work *L'homme machine* (*Man a Machine*).
