**David Samuel Tucker** is a geologist, author, and union organizer in [Washington](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington_(state)) state. He is a research associate at Western Washington University. He was an instructor at North Cascades Institute, and the director of the Mount Baker Volcano Research Center (now closed). He writes the blog *Northwest Geology Field Trips*, a blog aimed at laypeople detailing where to find interesting geology in the [Pacific Northwest](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pacific_Northwest).â€Š In 2015, he published a popular book on Washington geology, *Geology Underfoot in Western Washington*. He resides in [Bellingham](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bellingham,_Washington), Washington. In the 1980s he worked as a mountaineering guide in the [Cascades](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cascade_Range), [Mexico](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mexico), and [South America](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/South_America).
