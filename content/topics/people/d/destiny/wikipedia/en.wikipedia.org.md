  **Steven Kenneth Bonnell II** (born December 12, 1988), known online as **Destiny**, is an American live streamer and political commentator. He was among the first people to stream video games online full-time. Since 2016, he has streamed political debates with other online personalities, in which he advocates for and liberal and social democratic politics.
