  **Oskar Morgenstern** (January 24, 1902 – July 26, 1977) was a German-born economist. In collaboration with mathematician [John von Neumann](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_von_Neumann/), he founded the mathematical field of game theory as applied to the social sciences and strategic decision-making (see von Neumann–Morgenstern utility theorem).
