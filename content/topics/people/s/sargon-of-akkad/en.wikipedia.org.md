  **Sargon of Akkad** (/ˈsɑːrɡɒn/; Akkadian: 𒊬𒊒𒄀, romanized: *Šarrugi*), also known as **Sargon the Great**, was the first ruler of the [Akkadian Empire](/en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Akkadian_Empire/), known for his conquests of the Sumerian city-states in the 24th to 23rd centuries BC. He is sometimes identified as the first person in recorded history to rule over an empire.
