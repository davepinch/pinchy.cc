  **Huyen "Steven" Cheung** (born June 23, 1982) is an American political advisor serving as former President Donald Trump's campaign spokesman in the 2024 United States presidential election. He previously worked in Trump's 2016 and 2020 campaigns. He also worked in communications for the sports organization Ultimate Fighting Championship based in Las Vegas, Nevada.
