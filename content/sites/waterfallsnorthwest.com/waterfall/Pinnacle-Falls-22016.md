  Found along a headwater stream of Wells Creek. The falls, which drop about 165 feet in at least seven distinct steps (likely more), are almost impossible to view due to the twisting shape of the canyon they are located within. We attempted to survey this waterfall in 2013, but were unable to get any meaningful data or photographs due to the inhospitable shape of the canyon.
