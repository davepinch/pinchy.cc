  Middle Fork Nooksack Falls is the largest and most significant waterfall found along its namesake river (below the toe of the Deming Glacier at least) on Mount Baker. The falls occur about 2 miles downstream of the toe of the Deming Glacier where the river plunges over a step of volcanic bedrock in the valley floor and thunders a total of 81 feet in steps of 16 and 66 feet respectively...
