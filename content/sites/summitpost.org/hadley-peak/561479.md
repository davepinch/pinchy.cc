excerpt: >-
  Hadley Peak is a rather low-prominence peak (~400 feet) on the north flank of Mt. Baker where Chowder Ridge and Dobbs cleaver meet. The views are great, the peak just doesn’t stand out from a distance due to always having Baker in the background from all viewpoints. The wide Hadley Glacier spans the North flank of the peak, draining into Dobbs Creek.
