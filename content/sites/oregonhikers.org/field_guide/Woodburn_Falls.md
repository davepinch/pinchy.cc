title: "Woodburn Falls (OregonHikers.org)"
excerpt: >-
  Pretty 20-foot Woodburn Falls is an officially-named yet little known waterfall in Lacamas Regional Park. It is seasonal, becoming a waterfall around October rains and completely drying up by the end of June or early July. Despite its being seasonal, it is the only waterfall in Lacamas Lake Park that is not controlled by a dam, making the water flow 100% natural.
