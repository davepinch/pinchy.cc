excerpt: >-
  Seventy-seven acre Daybreak Park fronts the East Fork Lewis River in rural Clark County. There’s a more developed side of the park on the east side of 82nd Avenue with picnic tables, a play area, and restrooms. On the west side of 82nd, there’s a boat launch but also a trail loop that circles a big meadow and allows access to the cobbled shore of the river.
