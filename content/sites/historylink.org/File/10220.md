  Langley -- often referred to as the “Village by the Sea” -- is a South Whidbey Island town situated on a bluff overlooking Saratoga Passage and the Cascade Mountains. Located in Island County, it is a 10-minute drive from Clinton and a 20-minute ferry ride from Clinton to Mukilteo, the mainland ferry connecting point.
