✂️: "**Yacolt**: The Indian word Yacolt means “place abounding in evil spirits” or “haunted valley,” derived from an incident in which five Indian children were lost while picking wild berries, the demon, Yacolt, had gotten them, so the story goes! Two post offices competed in the area, Yacolt and Garner. They were combined under the present name."
