title: "route of Denny Mountain (wta.org)"
✂️: >-
  The basic route is up to top of Sessel chairlift, traverse over to Armstrong, then up Edelweiss Bowl up to top of Edelweiss chairlift. At the top of the chairlift take a trail up to the base of the summit block. Either go up vertical chimney, or go behind / thru the tree to the left, and then traverse around on an exposed ledge to the final scramble up to the summit.
