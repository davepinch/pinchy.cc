  The Cowiche Unit is an excellent example of intact shrubsteppe plant community attracting a variety of shrubsteppe obligate birds. Birders and nature lovers can see many bird species and beautiful spring wildflowers throughout the area. Elk and mule deer can be found year-round. This unit is walk-in and horseback access ONLY.
