---
title: "incomplete list"
note: >-
  This means the list has known members that are not documented. Note that the lack of 'incomplete list' does not mean a list is complete. In some cases lists are never completed (such as the list of cities) because the list would be unmanageable. When you see 'incomplete list', it means there are known members that should be added. Consider this a type of TODO.
