---
title: "Property owners are permitted to require AI de-anonymization as a condition of entry."
next: "Visitors have the right to refuse consent to AI de-anonymization."
---
Example:

  * A HOA (home owners association) that owns a private park may use an AI fence to ensure that all visitors are members or approved guests of members.

  * A tenant agreement may stipulate that all common spaces inside a building are subject to AI de-anonymization.
  
  * A music venue may collect explicit consent to AI de-anonymization as part of a ticket purchase.

  * A government agency may post a prominent sign that voluntarily entering a parking lot is considered consent to de-anonymization (with the specific statute cited).

  * A home owner may post a sign that any visitors approaching the door will be de-anonymized.
